CORRECTIONS TO DAILY HANSARD

The Draft Hansard is uncorrected. It is also privileged. Members have one ‘
week from the date of this issue of Draft Hansard in which to make
corrections to their speeches. Until the expiration of this one week period,
Draft Hansard must not be quoted as final and accurate report of the
debates of the National Parliament.

Corrections maybe marked on a photocopy of the Daily Draft Hansard and
lodged at the Office of the Principal Parliamentary Reporter, A1-23 (next
to the Security Control Room).

Corrections should be authorized by Signature and contain the Name,
Office and Telephone Number of the person transmitting/making
corrections.

Amendments cannot be accepted over the phone.

Corrections should relate only to inaccuracies. New matters may not be
introduced.

THIRD DAY
Thursday 16 March 2023
The Acting Speaker (Mr Koni Iguan) took the Chair at 10 a.m, and invited Reverend

Bernard Siai, Moderator of the United Church representing Papua New Guinea Council of

Churches, to say Prayers:
‘God is the King of all the earth, sing to him a psalm of praise. God reigns over the
nations and is seated on his holy throne. The nobles of the nations assemble, as the
people of the God of Abraham, for the kings of the earth belongs to God, and he is
greatly exalted.
Almighty God, we thank you that you are the King of this universe. Every leader
that is exalted by nations, they worship you and declare that, God, you are above
every god. This morning, Father we come in your presence knowing that as a nation
and our leaders, we have fallen short of your glory in many ways.
This morning, in this special occasion, we pray Father in the name of Jesus, that you
forgive us as your people. With the Blood of Jesus, cleanse us so that in this special
occasion of the dedication of our Governor-General, we do it with a righteous heart
so that your blessings will be upon our Governor-General.
Bless this hour of prayer and our dedication. We pray that the Holy Spirit will be in
the midst of us and our leaders, as we together as a nation with our leaders, dedicate
our Governor-General.
Holy Spirit, we ask you to fill our Governor-General, Sir Bob Dadae with wisdom,
understanding, discernment and knowledge. Father, we pray that you grant him your
favor, increase his faith and prepare him to pursue your excellence. Draw him to you
and cause him to know and fulfill your purposes. God, we pray that you also fill him
with sound and godly counsel, for without your counsel, he will not be able to
perform his duties as expected by our leaders and our nation as a whole.
We pray Father, that you will impart your wisdom and revelation to him, as he
performs his role as the Governor-General of Papua New Guinea. Keep his heart and
motives pure, willing and obedient to your will and in what he does, may he glorify
your name.

02/03
God, we thank you for Sir Bob Dadae. We thank you that he is one more time
appointed for second term knowing that he has that leadership skill and he is able to
perform in that particular office.

PRESENCE AND INTRODUCTION OF HIS
HONOUR THE CHIEF JUSTICE
Mr ACTING SPEAKER - Honourable Members, I wish to inform the Parliament that
His Honour, the Chief Justice of Papua New Guinea, Chief Sir Gibuna Gibbs Salika, GCL,
KBE, CSM, OBE, is in the precincts of the Parliament. With the concurrence of Honourable
Members, I invite His Honour the Chief Justice of Papua New Guinea to be seated on the
Floor of the Parliament.
PRESENCE AND INTRODUCTION OF HIS EXCELLENCY
THE GOVERNOR-GENERAL DESIGNATE
Sergeant-At-Arms announced the arrival of His Excellency the Governor-General
designate of Papua New Guinea.
03/03
Mr ACTING SPEAKER - Honourable Members, I have to advise that in accordance
with section 90 (1) of the Constitution, the Governor-General is required before entering upon
the duties of his office to take the Oath of Allegiance and to make the Declaration of Loyalty
and Office before the Chief Justice and in the presence of the Parliament.
I will now ask His Honour, the Chief Justice of Papua New Guinea, Sir Gibuna Gibbs
Salika, GCL, KBE, CSM, OBE to perform the Constitutional duties of his Office.

OATH OF ALLEGIANCE —-
DECLARATION OF OFFICE AND LOYALTY

The Governor-General designate, Sir Bob Dadae, took the Oath of Allegiance and
made the Declaration of Loyalty and Office.
04/03

Mr ACTING SPEAKER — Your Excellency, on behalf of the Members of Parliament
and the people of Papua New Guinea whom they represent, I offer you our congratulations
and wish you every success in the high office you now occupy.

Mr RAINBO PAITA (Finschhafen-Minister for Finance and Planning) — Mr Acting
Speaker, thank you. I thank members of Parliament for coming in full numbers to pay respect
to the swearing-in of our Excellency, Governor-General, which is a historic moment because
we’ ve voted him for the second time to be in office by both sides of the House.

05/03
