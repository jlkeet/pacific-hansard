Tuesday 14 March 2023

The Parliament met at 2p.m according to the Terms of Resolution of 19 January 2023. The Assistant Speaker, (Mr Johnson Wapunai) took the Chair.

There being no quorum present, he stated that he would resume the Chair after the ringing of the Bells.

The Acting Speaker (Mr Koni Iguan) took the Chair at 2.45 p.m. and invited the Governor for Chimbu, Honourable Noah Kool to say Prayers.

'Creator God, thou art in Heaven we praise you and honour you for the gift and sustainability of our lives. Despite the bad and sinful things that we do consciously or unconsciously on a daily basis, you still love us. We thank you for the mandate to represent our people and continue to give us wisdom and understanding so that we provide the leadership that you want. Today in this Parliament session and for the next two weeks and in the future, take lead and take charge and take control, Lord we trust in you; Amen.'
