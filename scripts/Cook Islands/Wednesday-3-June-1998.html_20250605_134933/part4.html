Maybe the Ombudsman would be able to shed some light on some areas of this Report. We in the Pacific are aware of the Ombudsman of Vanuatu who has featured quite regularly in the media over the last couple of years. We would've read in the newspapers recently that the Parliament of Vanuatu has accepted a Parliamentary Code and I believe this was greatly influenced by their Ombudsman. That is with regard to a Leadership Code for Parliament. Therefore I would like to ask the Minister again if it would be possible to contact and ask the Ombudsman of Vanuatu to be present because that particular Ombudsman might be able to give us an insight into how we might be able to do better things for our country with regard to this review of our Ombudsman. Thank you.
