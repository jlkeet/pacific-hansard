Before we finish I wish to thank the Prime Minister for the change in the way the Ceremonial Opening of the Forty-Second Session of Parliament is to be conducted. It was witnessed and was appreciated by many. The first message that I received the opening would be done in an ordinary way. But the second message reminded me of this very wise saying and it goes like this “Yea, winter has passed, the rain has stopped, the land is covered with the blossoms of flowers, and the music from the singing doves reached our ears.”
Can an Honourable Member please close us with a prayer?
