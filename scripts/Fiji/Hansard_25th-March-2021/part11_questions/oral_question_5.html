COVID-19 Biosecurity Measures (Question No. 77/2021)

HON. S.R. RASOVA asked the Government, upon notice:

Parliament on the COVID-19 biosecurity measures at our ports of entry?

HON. SPEAKER.- We will relook at that question at some future time, so we will move on

to the next question. I hope the next person is ready.
