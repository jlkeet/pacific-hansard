which they have paid for at $4.22. Before COVID-19, the share price in 2019 in Fiji Airways was
$16 a share. They have now bought it at $4.22 a share. With Fiji Airways flying the way it is with
the borders being reopened within two years they will be able to double or triple the share value.

same way they made some smart investment in EFL.

Nauru 0.031 percent.
