ELECTION OF LEADER OF THE OPPOSITION
HON. A.A. MAHARAJ.- Mr. Speaker, I move:
That honourable Josaia Voreqe Bainimarama be appointed as Leader of the Opposition.
HON. I.B. SERUIRATU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I beg to second the motion.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, there being no further nominations, I, therefore,
declare honourable Josaia Voreqe Bainimarama elected as Leader of the Opposition.
(Acclamation)
