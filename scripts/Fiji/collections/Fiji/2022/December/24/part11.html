ELECTION OF DEPUTY SPEAKER
HON. L.D. TABUYA.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That honourable Lenora Qereqeretabua do take the position of Deputy Speaker of Parliament.
HON. C.J. SINGH.- I beg to second the motion.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, are there any further nominations?
HON. A.A. MAHARAJ.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That honourable Viliame Naupoto do take the position of Deputy Speaker of Parliament.
HON. I.B. SERUIRATU.- I beg to second the motion.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, since there is more than one nomination, we shall
proceed with the casting of votes by secret ballot.
[Ballot papers distributed to Members by chamber attendants]
Honourable Members, you are requested to write the name of the person whom you wish to
vote for on the ballot paper and fold it so that the name written on it cannot be seen. The Chamber
Attendants will collect your ballot papers for counting at the Table.
Pursuant to Standing Order 19(7), I call upon the honourable Alvick Maharaj and honourable
Lynda Tabuya, to come forward and act as scrutineers and observe the count at the table.
[Ballot papers collected and counted in the presence of scrutineers]
Honourable Members, the result of the secret ballot are as follows:

Qereqeretabua elected as the Deputy Speaker of the Parliament of the Republic of Fiji.
