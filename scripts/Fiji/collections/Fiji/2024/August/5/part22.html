Response to Question No. 112/2024 is as follows:
Mr Speaker Sir, I thank Hon Jone Usamate for raising the question on the list of Capital
projects budgeted for FY2024/2025 by Fiji Roads Authority, its anticipated completion dates and
the benefits and beneficiaries of each project.
The Fiji Roads Authority (FRA) received a Capital Budget of $338,766,421 for the
FY2024/2025. The programmes that form part of the Capital budget include Maintenance &
Renewals (71 percent), New Capital (18 percent), and Transport Infrastructure Investment
Sector Program (11 percent).
