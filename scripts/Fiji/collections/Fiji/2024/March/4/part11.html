ADJOURNMENT
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 11th March, 2024 at 9.30 a.m.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, the second session of the third term of the Fijian
Parliament has been officially opened by His Excellency the President. All Parliamentary business
including Parliamentary sittings and Parliamentary Standing Committee proceedings on Bills,
Motions and Reports will resume. I think I am a bit late in announcing this. Some letters are on my
table already from the Standing Committees and they are eager to move on. I take this opportunity
to thank you all for your presence on this auspicious occasion, as well as those joining us through the
live broadcast of the proceedings on television and the internet.
At the next Parliament Sitting, Parliament will proceed with a motion by the honourable
Prime Minister to thank His Excellency for his most gracious Speech. All honourable Members will
speak in accordance with the Batting Order that has been circulated.
On that note, I thank honourable Members, and I now invite you all to refreshments which
will be served at the Big Committee Room.
Honourable Members, Parliament now stands adjourned until Monday, 11th March, 2024 at
9.30 a.m.
The Parliament adjourned at 11.13 a.m.
