ADJOURNMENT
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 2nd December, 2024 at 9.30 a.m.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, I second the motion.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, I now declare Parliament adjourned until Monday,
2nd December, 2024 at 9.30 a.m.
The Parliament adjourned at 8.34 a.m.
