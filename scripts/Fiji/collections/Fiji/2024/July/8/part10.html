Welcome
I welcome all honourable Members to today’s sitting. I also welcome those joining us in the
gallery and those watching the live proceedings on television and the internet. Thank you for your
interest in this week’s National Budget debate.
His Excellency the Australian High Commissioner
Honourable Members, it will be remiss of me if I do not acknowledge the Australian High
Commissioner, His Excellency Ewen McDonald. Thank you for joining us this morning, Your
Excellency.
Time Allocation on Debate
For the information of honourable Members, the Business Committee, in its meeting on
Wednesday, 26th June, 2024, and in addition to the Communications on Friday, 28th June, 2024, had
unanimously agreed to the following:
(1) The Shadow Minister for Finance (honourable Leader of the Opposition), in delivering the
response to the Budget Address, will speak for the same amount of time as the honourable
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance in delivering the National Budget Address.
(2) All remaining Government Members will have up to 10 minutes to respond to the Budget
Address, with the exception of the honourable and learned Attorney-General, who will have
20 minutes as he will be delivering his Maiden Speech.
(3) All remaining Opposition Members will have up to 15 minutes to respond to the Budget
Address.
