MR. CHAIRMAN.- SEG 3?
HON. P.D. KUMAR.- I have a question on SEG 3(3) on Official Overseas Travel Cost. Just
a question whether this is the only cost or some amounts are also in different ministries for their
ministerial travel?
HON. PROF. B.C. PRASAD.- This is a pool allocation which caters for air fare cost and per
diem allowances for Ministers and Assistant Ministers travelling abroad for official duties.
HON. P.D. KUMAR.- My question is not answered. Is this the only amount or travel is also
included in each Ministry head?
HON. PROF. B.C. PRASAD.- There is travel in ministry head but for overseas travel, it is
from Head 50.
HON. P.D. KUMAR.- Why I am asking because I recall asking this question to the
honourable Minister for Education when the travel vote increased by $450,000 and he also said that
it was for the overseas visits by the honourable Minister as well as by the officials. So, I just wanted
that clarification.
HON. PROF. B.C. PRASAD.- It is overseas travel for Ministers.
