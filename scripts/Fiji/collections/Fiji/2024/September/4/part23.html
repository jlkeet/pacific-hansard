Reply to Question No. 184/2024 is as follows:
Mr. Speaker, Sir, I thank the honourable Member for the question. A total of 19 Health
Facilities in our rural and maritime areas have been earmarked for upgrade in this financial year.
They are as follows:
(1) Eastern Division – 5 Facilities
Kadavu Hospital, Levuka Hospital, Rotuma Hospital, Lakeba Hospital, Vanua Balavu
Hospital.
(2) Western Division – 6 Facilities
Nacula Health Centre, Yalobi/Yaqeta, Naqalimare Nursing Station, Raiwaqa Health Centre,
Tuvu Nursing Station and Tokaimalo Nursing Station.
(3) Central Division – 3 Facilities
Tonia Nursing Station, Korovou Nursing Station and Nayavu Nursing Station.
(4) Northern Division – 5 Facilities
Naqumu Nursing Station, Kubulau Health Centre, Dreketi Health Centre, Tawake Nursing
Station and Qamea Health Centre.
Mr. Speaker, Sir, the Ministry has continued with its health infrastructure improvement
programme with the budgetary allocation from Government and together with the generous support
of our partners, local businesses, civil society organisations, hospital board of visitors and community
members.
