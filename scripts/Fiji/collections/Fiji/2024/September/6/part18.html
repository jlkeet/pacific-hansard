ADJOURNMENT
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, I move:
That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 30th September, 2024, at 9.30 a.m.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, I second the motion.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, that concludes the end of our sitting for this week.
I must thank you all for your contributions throughout the week. I now declare Parliament adjourned
until Monday, 30th September, 2024, at 9.30 a.m.
The Parliament adjourned at 3.29 p.m.
