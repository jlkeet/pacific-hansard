ADJOURNMENT
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Madam Deputy Speaker, I
move:
That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 2nd December, 2024 at 9.30 a.m.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Madam Deputy Speaker, I second the motion.
DEPUTY SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, Parliament will now vote on the motion.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
DEPUTY SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, that concludes the end of the Special Sitting
and I thank you all for your participation this morning.
Honourable Members, Ratu Naiqama Tawakecolati Lalabalavu is President-Elect and does
not assume the Constitutional role until he takes office on the day he swears the Oath for President.
That being the case, the Speaker will remain in office until 11th November, 2024. The Office of the
Speaker will be deemed to fall vacant on 12th November, 2024, and pursuant to Standing Order 17,
the vacancy will be filled on Monday, 2nd December, 2024.
