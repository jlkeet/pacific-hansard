SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Thank you Mr. Speaker,
Sir, I move:
Under Standing Order 6 that so much of Standing Orders 23(1) is suspended so as to
allow the Parliament to sit beyond 4.30 p.m. today to complete the remaining items listed on
the Order Paper.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, we still have
the current debate and two more motions to be debated. There have been indications of several
speakers, so I am asking Parliament to support that we move and sit beyond 4.30 p.m. in order to
cover the remaining items.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
