HON. S.L. RABUKA.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, before I table my Report please, allow me to
express, on behalf of the Government and I suppose, on behalf of the Opposition, and every Member
of this august Parliament, to extend to you our most sincere condolences in the passing of one
illustrious member of your chiefly family of A i Sokula, Ratu Epeli Ganilau. He was a noble
representative of the family, an illustrious member of the vanua o Cakadrove and a distinguished
Military leader. We pray that he will rest in God’s eternal peace.
