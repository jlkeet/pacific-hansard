COMMUNICATION FROM THE SPEAKER
I welcome all honourable Members to today’s sitting and all those joining us in the gallery,
as well as those watching the live proceedings on television and the internet. Thank you for taking
an interest in your Parliament.
Honourable Members, please, join me in welcoming another group of PL100 Students from
the University of the South Pacific. I also welcome other members of the public in the gallery,
especially the former Member of Parliament, Mr. Peceli Rinakama. I hope that you will enjoy and
also learn something from today’s proceedings.
