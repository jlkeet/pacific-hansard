SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS
HON. RO. F.Q. TUISAWAU. – Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That under Standing Order 6, that so much of Standing Order 23(1) is
suspended, so as to allow the Parliament to sit beyond 4:30 p.m. today to complete the
remaining items listed on today’s Order Paper. Thank you.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.
HON. RO. F.Q. TUISAWAU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, we still have Schedule 2 - Motions for
Debate, about five and of course, the Bills, before Parliament. Thank you.
MR. SPEAKER.- Thank you. Honourable Members, the floor is now open for debate on the
motion. If any, I now call on the honourable Leader of Government, in Parliament to speak in reply,
if any.
HON. RO. F.Q. TUISAWAU.- Thank you, Mr. Speaker, Sir. I do not have any further
comments to add. Vinaka.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, I intend to adjourn Parliament now for afternoon
tea, before we continue. Parliament is adjourned.
The Parliament adjourned at 3.54 p.m.
