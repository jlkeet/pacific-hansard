SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move
under Standing Order 6:
That so much of Standing Orders 23(1) is suspended so as to allow the Parliament to sit
beyond 4.30 p.m. today to complete the remaining items listed on today’s Order Paper.
HON. A.A. MAHARAJ.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, very
briefly under Schedule 1, we still have an item on today’s Order Paper and, of course, the six Oral
Questions under Schedule 2 and one Written Question, thus the request to sit beyond 4.30 p.m.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
