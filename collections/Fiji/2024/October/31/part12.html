ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF THE APPOINTMENT OF THEPRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF FIJI

ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF THE APPOINTMENT OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF FIJI DEPUTY SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, the results of the roll call votes are as follows: 1) Nominee of the Prime Minister, Ratu Naiqama Tawakecolati Lalabalavu - 37 votes; 2) Nominee of the Leader of the Opposition, Ratu Meli Natalatu Tora Tavaiqia - 16 votes. Honourable Members, pursuant to Section 84(3) of the Constitution of the Republic of Fiji and Standing Order 136 (6), I now declare Ratu Naiqama Tawakecolati Lalabalavu, having received the votes of more than half of all the Members present, appointed as the President of the Republic of Fiji. (Acclamation) Honourable Members, at this juncture, I wish to convey, on behalf of Parliament, our warmest congratulations to the Turaga Bale Na Tui Cakau, Ratu Naiqama Tawakecolati Lalabalavu, on his appointment as the President-elect of the Republic of Fiji. I am confident that he will continue the good work of his predecessors, and I wish him the very best in his tenure as custodian of the Office of the President. I also take this opportunity to thank you all, honourable Members, for your participation in this important Constitutional process.
