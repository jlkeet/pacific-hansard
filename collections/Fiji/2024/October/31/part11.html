NOMINATIONS FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF FIJI DEPUTY SPEAKER.- I now call upon the honourable Prime Minister for his nomination. HON. S. L. RABUKA.- Madam Deputy Speaker, pursuant to Section 84 of the Constitution of the Republic of Fiji: I hereby nominate Ratu Naiqama Tawakecolati Lalabalavu to be appointed as the President of the Republic of Fiji. DEPUTY SPEAKER.- I now call upon the honourable Leader of the Opposition for his nomination. HON. I. B. SERUIRATU.- Madam Deputy Speaker, pursuant to Section 84 of the Constitution of the Republic of Fiji: I hereby nominate Ratu Meli Natalatu Tora Tavaiqia to be appointed as President of the Republic of Fiji. DEPUTY SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, there being more than one nomination, please, take note that Parliament shall now proceed to the roll call vote. Pursuant to Standing Order 136(4), the Secretary-General will ask each honourable Member separately on how the Member wishes to vote. Each Member, upon being called, will give his or her vote, stating the name of the person for whom the Member wishes to vote. Honourable Members, pursuant to Standing Order 136(5), and after tallying the results, the Secretary-General will read out the results of the vote. Please, take further note that the roll call vote will be undertaken in alphabetical order of surnames. Secretary-General, please, proceed with the roll call vote.
