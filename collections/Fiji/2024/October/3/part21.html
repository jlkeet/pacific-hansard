ADJOURNMENT

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, I move: That Parliament adjourns until such date and time as will be determined in due course for a special sitting to appoint the President of the Republic of Fiji.

HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.

Question put. Motion agreed to. The Parliament adjourned sine die at 7.46 p.m.
