HON. M. S. N. KAMIKAMICA.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, pursuant to Standing Order 51, I move: That the - (a) Access to Business Funding Bill 2024 (Bill No. 18/2024), be considered by Parliament without delay; (b) Bill must pass through one stage at a single sitting of Parliament; (c) Bill must be immediately referred to the Standing Committee on Economic Affairs; (d) Standing Committee must report on the Bill at the next sitting of Parliament upon

presentation of the Report on the Bill by the Standing Committee; and

(e) Bill must be debated and voted upon by Parliament at the next sitting of Parliament, with the right of reply given to me as the Member moving this motion.
