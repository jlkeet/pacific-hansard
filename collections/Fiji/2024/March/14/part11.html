MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, whatever you say in Parliament can be addressed under Standing Orders, but if you go to the social media, that is beyond my reach. But I certainly would like to ask honourable Ketan Lal, for the sake of ensuring that we maintain the dignity of the Parliament. Honourable Ketan Lal, I am talking, for the sake of preserving the dignity of Parliament, I ask that in your own good time, please, ensure that whatever is being stated in the social media should not paint that picture of you continuing your diatribe outside of Parliament when full explanations have been given. I am not here to defend the honourable Minister, but his dad, he has also served his time well for Fiji in undertaking duties bestowed to him by the honourable Prime Minister now, and we even served time together. So, that is just one of the things that affects us one way or another since we come from the islands. His sons have represented the country and I was his running mate in the 1999 General Elections. That, in a nutshell, is just a short explanation of this late great parliamentarian and gentleman. He is a huge man, honourable Member, you can see it by the built of his sons; not this one here.
