ADJOURNMENT

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr: Speaker, I move: That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 30 th September, 2024, at 9.30 a.m.

HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr: Speaker, I second the motion.

Question put. Motion agreed to. MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, that concludes the end of our sitting for this week. I must thank you all for your contributions throughout the week.
