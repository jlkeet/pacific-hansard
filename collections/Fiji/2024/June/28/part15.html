28 th June, 2024 MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, and our distinguished guests present in the gallery today, that brings us to the end of our sitting today. I trust that all honourable Members will utilise the coming week to prepare well for the Budget Debate week. On that note, I thank the honourable Deputy Prime Minister and the Minister of Finance and Strategic Planning, National Development and Statistics for delivering the 2024-2025 National Budget Address. I thank all honourable Members for your forbearance. I also thank all our invited guests in the gallery and those watching the live proceedings. At this juncture, I extend an invitation to all our invited guests in the gallery to join the honourable Members in the Big Committee Room for refreshment.
