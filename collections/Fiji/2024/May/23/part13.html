The total funding, Mr. Speaker, Sir, that is expected to be part of the three approaches, particularly the two approaches will amount to about $294 million. The approach to formulating the strategy, Mr. Speaker, Sir, was based on extensive stakeholder engagement. There were 11 thematic workshops with a total of 229 participants from Government agencies, civil society organisations, development partners, field consultations at national, divisional, provincial, district and community levels were conducted with another 300 participants.
