Time Allocation on Debate For the information of honourable Members, the Business Committee, in its meeting on Wednesday, 26 th June, 2024, and in addition to the Communications on Friday, 28 th June, 2024, had unanimously agreed to the following: (1) The Shadow Minister for Finance (honourable Leader of the Opposition), in delivering the response to the Budget Address, will speak for the same amount of time as the honourable Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance in delivering the National Budget Address. (2) All remaining Government Members will have up to 10 minutes to respond to the Budget Address, with the exception of the honourable and learned Attorney-General, who will have 20 minutes as he will be delivering his Maiden Speech.

(3) All remaining Opposition Members will have up to 15 minutes to respond to the Budget

Address.
