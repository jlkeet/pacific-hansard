SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT. - Mr: Speaker, Sir, I move: Under Standing Order 6, that so much of Standing Order 23(1) is suspended so as to allow Parliament to sit beyond 4.30 p.m. today to complete the remaining items listed on today’s Order Paper.

HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, we have Schedule 2 – Motions for Debate, as you can see in the Agenda, to cover which is why we are asking for the motion in front of us. MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, the floor is now open for debate, if any. HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I have no further comments to add. Question put. Motion agreed to.
