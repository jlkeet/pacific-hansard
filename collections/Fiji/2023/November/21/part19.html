SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, I move t under Standing Order 6: That so much of Standing Order 23(1) is suspended so as to allow Parliament to sit beyond 4.30 p.m. today to complete the remaining items listed on today’s Order Paper.

HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.: I second the motion, Mr. Speaker, Sir.

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, we have Schedule 2 - Motions for Debate, there are still a few motions there in terms of extending the time. MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, the floor is now open for debate on the motion, if any. HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- I have no further comments to make, Mr. Speaker, Sir. Question put. Motion agreed to. MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, I intend to adjourn Parliament for tea break. The Parliament adjourned at 3.59 p.m.
