Clause 1 of the Bill provides for the short title and commencement. If passed by Parliament, the amending legislation will come into force on a date or dates appointed by the Minister by notice in the Gazette. Clause 5 of the Bill amends section 5 of the Act to empower the Minister to make regulations in relation to the GCC. Clause 6 of the Bill amends section 11 of the Act to require the Minister to refer the Board or the Council any proposed Bill that is intended to be introduced in Parliament and which may appear to the Minister to affect in any important matter the rights and interests of iTaukei people.
