HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I move:
That Parliament be adjourned to Monday, 13th February, 2023 at 9.30 a.m.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.

3rd February, 2023
Motion agreed to.
MR. SPEAKER.- Honourable Members, the 2023 Parliament Session has been officially
opened by His Excellency the President. I take this opportunity to thank you all for your presence
on this auspicious occasion, as well as those joining us through the live broadcast of the proceedings
on television and internet. It would be remiss of me if I am not to thank the hard work undertaken
by Madam Secretary-General and the staff in preparing this auspicious occasion. It has been a tiring
task working late into the night preparing this great occasion.
At the next Parliament sitting, Parliament will proceed with the Administration of Oath, and
this will be followed by a motion by the honourable Prime Minister to thank His Excellency for his
most gracious speech. All honourable Members will speak in accordance with the Batting Order that
has been circulated. On that note, I thank honourable Members, and I now invite you all to
refreshments which will be served at the Big Committee Room.
Parliament now stands adjourned until Monday, 13th February, 2023 at 9.30 a.m.
The Parliament adjourned at 11.09 a.m.
