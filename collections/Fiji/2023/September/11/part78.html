In December 2017, School Heads, School Management, Divisional and District Offices were
informed via Circular (C103-17), that secondary schools were to be classified as Small,
Medium or Large based on the number of teaching staff in the school.
SMALL secondary schools were classified as those schools that had 25 teachers or less with
a Principal and VP included and an AP only if the school had more than 180 students;
MEDIUM secondary schools were classified as those schools with 26-50 teachers with
Principal, VP and AP included ; and LARGE secondary schools were classified as those
schools that had over 51 teachers with a Principal, VP and AP included.
Since 2017, Executive-approved changes to some schools' classifications have occurred . In
addition , many schools have been allocated additional teachers. As a result, under the current
' teacher-only' criteria, there has become a lack of equity and/or consistency for many
secondary schools within the secondary classifications system.
