ADJOURNMENT

HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT. - Mr: Speaker, I move: That Parliament adjourns until Monday, 15 th April, 2024 at 9.30 a.m.

HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr: Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.

Question put. Motion agreed to. MR. SPEAKER. - Honourable Members, that concludes the end of our sitting for this week. I thank you all for your contributions throughout the week.
