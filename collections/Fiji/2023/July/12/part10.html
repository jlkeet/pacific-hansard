Welcome
I welcome all honourable Members to today’s Sitting. I also understand that most have not
been feeling very well during the long sitting hours and I hope and pray that we will all be present
when we try and finalise the very, very important paper that is before us.
I also welcome those joining us in the gallery. I am told that there is a Dr. Jioji Tabudravu,
who is from Wainunu in Bua. He has a PhD and is a lecturer in Natural Products and Analytical
Chemistry at the University of Central Lancashire in the School of Natural Sciences in the United
Kingdom. Welcome, Sir.
