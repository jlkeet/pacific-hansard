MINUTES
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker,
before I confirm the Minutes of the sitting yesterday, I wish to beg your indulgence and
the indulgence of the House in the Budget response speech of the honourable Attorney-
General and Minister for Justice yesterday. He inadvertently referred to your goodself,
Sir, when he mentioned “Your Excellency the Speaker”. He had meant to say, “His
Excellency the President”. If that could be corrected, Mr. Speaker, in the Daily
Hansard, then we can proceed to confirm the Minutes. So, begging your indulgence and
the indulgence of the House before we confirm.
MR. SPEAKER.- Yes. I think we all agree to that as he accidently said that.
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Thank you, Mr.
Speaker, can I confirm the Minutes subject to the change?
MR. SPEAKER.- Yes.
HON. LEADER OF THE GOVERNMENT IN PARLIAMENT.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I
move:
That the Minutes of the sitting of Parliament held on Wednesday, 12th July, 2023
as previously circulated, be taken as read and be confirmed subject to the correction of
what was stated yesterday.
HON. A.V.B.C. BAINIVALU.- Mr. Speaker, Sir, I second the motion.
Question put.
Motion agreed to.
