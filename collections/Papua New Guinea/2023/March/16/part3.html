PRESENCE AND INTRODUCTION OF HIS

Mr ACTING SPEAKER - Honourable Members, I wish to inform the Parliament that

His Honour, the Chief Justice of Papua New Guinea, Chief Sir Gibuna Gibbs Salika, GCL,

KBE, CSM, OBE, is in the precincts of the Parliament. With the concurrence of Honourable

Members, I invite His Honour the Chief Justice of Papua New Guinea to be seated on the

Floor of the Parliament.
