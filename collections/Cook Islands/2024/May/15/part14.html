MRS S. TAPAITAU: “The Lord is my strength and my shield, my heart trusts in Him, and He helps me. My heart leaps for joy and with my song I praise Him.” Psalm 28:7.
Kia Orana in the Name of Our Lord and let us give thanks for His blessing in convening us in this Honourable House.
Honourable Speaker, Kia Orana. Kia Orana Clerk, Deputy Clerk, and all the hardworking staff of Parliament, and a special mention to my brother Papaterai. To the King’s Representative, Sir Tom Marsters and Lady Marsters, Kia Orana. The President of the House of Ariki and Koutu Nui, our Religious Advisory Council and all our respective Church Members and everyone listening in to our Session today, welcome to Parliament.
Kia Orana to the Prime Minister of our Nation, Honourable Mark Brown, Kia Orana to the Deputy Prime Minister Honourable Albert Nicholas, Honourable Ministers and all Members of Parliament on the government side. Kia Orana also to the Honourable Leader of the Opposition, Honourable Tina Pupuke Browne and all Members on your side. Kia Orana to the Leader of the United Party, Honourable Teariki Heather and Members of Parliament on your side. Furthermore, greetings to the Member of Parliament for One Cook Islands. Kia Orana to all Independent Members. Furthermore, Kia Orana to the Member of Parliament for the One Cook Islands Party and greetings to all the Independent Members of Parliament, the Honourable Minister Rose Brown and the Honourable Tehani Brown and the Honourable Stephen Matapo. May we all feel joyful and content on this beautiful day that God had bestowed upon us.
I wish now to send special regards to my beloved Tongareva people, whom I cherish deeply. Kia Orana to you all in the Mighty Name of our Lord. The three pillars that we always acknowledge on our island – the Church; both Church Ministers from both villages of Omoka and Tetautua, Mama Assistant Minister and also our Catholic Church. Those in higher positions and everyone else in our Churches, Kia Orana to you all in the Name of our Lord. To our respected traditional leaders, including the Mayor, Deputy Mayor, Council Members and your families. May God grant you the wisdom and understanding to lead His people on the right path that suits Him. To the government, including the Executive Officer, all the different government departments, and every employee who serves in them.
To my dear people of Tongareva, thank you for entrusting me with the responsibility of serving as your Member of Parliament. It is an honour to be your eyes and voice inside this Honourable House and I pledge to represent your interest with diligence and integrity.
As we navigate through the challenges that lie ahead, I promise to lead our people with determination and commitment, guiding our canoe with the crown of leadership that you have

I would like to pay tributes to my parents, Papa Tini Ford, rest in peace Dad. The late Reverend Oirua Rasmussen and Mama Tongi and Mama Miera. Forever, in our hearts. To my role model my dearest mother, may God bless and protect you and grant you a long healthy life in His service and in serving our people.
To my families, thank you for everything and to my children, thank you for all your support and love. To my husband, Robert Tapaitau, love you always.
