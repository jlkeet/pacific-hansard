OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): Please be seated. I call the House to Order. Kia Orana to everyone this afternoon. Kia Orana to our people listening in from the Outer Islands including Rarotonga. Kia Orana to the Honourable Prime Minister, Deputy Prime Minister, Honourable Ministers and Honourable Members on the Government side. Kia Orana to the Leader of the Opposition, the Honourable Tina Pupuke Browne and Members on your side. Kia Orana also to the Leader of the United Party and Leader of the One Cook Islands Party as well. Kia Orana also to our guests in the Public Gallery, welcome to this Honourable House.
Pursuant to SO 48 (1) (c), and in accordance with Article 30 of the Constitution, our first business this afternoon is the swearing in of Mrs Sarakura Tapaitau. Mrs Tapaitau was the successful candidate in the Penrhyn by-election held on 12 March 2024.
I call upon Mrs Sarakura Tapaitau for the swearing in to come forward.
(Mrs Sarakura Tapaitau sworn)
