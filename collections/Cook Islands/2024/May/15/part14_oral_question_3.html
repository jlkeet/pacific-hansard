Oral Question 3

MR DEPUTY SPEAKER (MR T. TANGATAPOTO): Honourable Members, before I begin my official duty as Deputy Speaker of this House, I would like to take this opportunity to thank the Honourable Prime Minister and all Cabinet Ministers and all the Members of the Government for putting your trust in me to take over the role of Deputy Speaker in this Honourable House.
I will assure you Mr Prime Minister and all Honourable Members of this House, I will do my job to the best of what is required of me, going forward. Now, we come to our Question Time for 30 minutes.
