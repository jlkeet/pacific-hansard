Oral Question 13

HON. A. NICHOLAS: MrDeputy Speaker, thank you for your question to the Prime Minister, but I would like to provide an answer if I may. If I understand your question correctly, you are inquiring about the incinerator. In terms of the procurement process, it is still ongoing at this time.
As part of the procurement process, a Request for Quotes was issued, both locally and overseas. Approximately 14 to 15 companies have expressed interest and submitted quotes.

Regarding funding for this project, I would like to clarify that it is sourced from the ICF under the New Zealand Government. Following an investigation, the New Zealand High Commission Office has been directed to allocate the necessary funds for the project.
I would also like to touch on the current situation at the landfill. As you may be aware, the government has contracted TNM's compress machine to compress the rubbish at the landfill using a roller. The compressed rubbish has allowed us to extend the lifespan of the landfill. I believe this answers your question.
