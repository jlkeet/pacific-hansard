Oral Question 12

MR T. VARU: Congratulations to you Mr Deputy Speaker, for leading our Session today. Kia Orana to all Members present in this Honourable House. We would also like to welcome our newly appointed Member and our sister.
I have a question to ask, which was initially posed by a Member of Parliament from Matavera to the Prime Minister at the beginning of 2023. If I recall, the question was focused on our waste management practices. I believe the Prime Minister, or maybe the Minister Responsible Honourable Albert Nicholas, responded during that Session, noting that the Ministry of Infrastructure was exploring various avenues to reduce waste, including acquiring a machine to burn rubbish. Perhaps the Honourable Prime Minister could provide a response to this question today?
