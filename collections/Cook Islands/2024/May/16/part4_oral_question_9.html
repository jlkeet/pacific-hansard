Oral Question 9

MR T. ISAMAELA: Honourable Minister, I have three questions that I would like to ask you. Firstly, why is there no government office in Aitutaki that can provide assistance to our elderly citizens? Secondly, why is there a prolonged delay in processing their applications? Finally, what has become of the three applications that were submitted on 6 January 2024, as there has been no response to them thus far? Once you have provided your responses, I may have additional inquiries to pose.
