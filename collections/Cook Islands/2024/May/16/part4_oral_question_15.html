Oral Question 15

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Good Afternoon to everyone. Kia Orana to all the Members present here including you, Mr Speaker, along with your staff, the Prime Minister, Cabinet Ministers, Members of Opposition, United Party, and Independent Party Representatives. I would also like to acknowledge the King's Representative and your spouse, who are listening to the broadcast. Kia Orana to the Kaumaiti and the House of Ariki, the Leaders of the Churches, and people from the Northern Group and Southern Group, both here in Rarotonga and abroad.
Mr Prime Minister, I have a question for you. Two groups from our country recently travelled to Fiji for a workshop, which I believe was an essential duty travel. During that period, I saw many tourists visiting Fiji and I believe, just like us, they are also trying to recover from the pandemic. I recollect reading in the Cook Islands news last year that we were exploring the possibility of an airline flying from Nadi to the Cook Islands to promote tourism. Could you please update us on where we stand concerning this matter at present?
