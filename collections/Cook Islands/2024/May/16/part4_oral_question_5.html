Oral Question 5

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you Honourable Member from Matavera for your question. As you mentioned God's Words, I would like to respond with another Bible Verse: "Seek and you will find; knock and the door will be opened to you." Therefore, if you send me your request, I will provide a response.
Regarding how I acquired my excavator, it was through the Chinese, after a request was made by George Angene. It is not true that the Government approved the machine, as some have falsely claimed.
In response to your request for an excavator for Matavera, I fully support it. Please visit my office, and we can collaborate to determine how to navigate this process. I would like to extend an invitation for you to visit my office, where I can provide you with a plan for obtaining a machine for your constituency. However, I must admit that I am uncertain about your proposal, as there are several Members of Parliament seeking access to similar resources that I have acquired. In this regard, I recommend that you also seek assistance from Deputy Prime Minister, Albert Nicholas.
I would like to reflect on the Bible Verse shared by our Chaplain today: "Do not judge, or you too will be judged," as stated in Matthew 7:1. The Verse reminds us that we should avoid judging
