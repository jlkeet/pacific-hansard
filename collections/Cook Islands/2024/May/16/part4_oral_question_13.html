Oral Question 13

MR R. HEATHER: Kia Orana to you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to the Clerk, Parliamentary staff, and all the Honourable Members, as well as to our country's leaders, such as the Leader of the Opposition, the Leader of our Party, other representatives, and traditional leaders. Kia Orana!
Mr Speaker, I have a brief question to ask the Minister for Internal Affairs regarding the tender process for cleaning our roads and villages on our island. I understand that the last tendering process took place in 2018 and since then, I have not received any updates on the tendering process. The reason for my inquiry is that I am aware that three individuals had submitted tenders for the cleaning of roads throughout the island, but I am unsure about the status of the applications.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you, Honourable Member. I see the Honourable Minister; Rose Toki- Brown; you have the Floor.
