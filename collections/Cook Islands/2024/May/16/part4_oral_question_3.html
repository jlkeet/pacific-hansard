Oral Question 3

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. My question is directed to the Minister of Cultural Development. Reflecting on the Daily Bread that our Chaplain shared with us earlier, "do not condemn, and you will not be condemned," I am curious to know how the Minister acquired his excavator. Specifically, I am keen to learn about the steps he took to procure it, as I believe this information can be useful in helping Matavera acquire one as well.
I am aware that many people have seen the Minister's excavator, and I commend him for successfully acquiring one. My request for the Minister is if he can provide any advice on how Matavera can obtain its own excavator. Thank you.
