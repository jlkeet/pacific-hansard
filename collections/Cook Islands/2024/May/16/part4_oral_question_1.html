Oral Question 1

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to you, every Member of this House, and to all those listening in to our Session today.
Yesterday, the Honourable Member from Akaoa posed a question to me regarding the $400 thousand that was allocated in the previous financial year for a foreshore protection proposal concept note. To save time during our Question Time, I have comprehensive documentation that I would like to hand over to the Honourable Member to read tonight before bed. The documentation clearly stipulates what has happened since the financial year budget was approved.
In summary, the process to approve the concept note for this program required a requested tender, which had to go through an RFQ and obtain approval from both the MFEM and Crown Law Office. Currently, the documentation is with Crown Law, but they have assured us that it will be ready by the end of this month, after which we can proceed with the tender. However, given that we are nearing the end of the financial year 2023/24, we will apply to carry forward this budget of $400 thousand into the 2024/25 financial year.
