Oral Question 12

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I wanted to ask a Supplementary Question to the Minister of Justice, but I will hold off until his report to Parliament is presented. My question is directed to the Minister of Corrective Services. While this isn't meant to be a praise, I have observed that the management of our Corrective Services is functioning effectively under your leadership. It seems that you are handling the responsibilities well. Given the challenges currently facing the Police Department, do you have any strategies or ideas for collaboration that could help improve their situation?
