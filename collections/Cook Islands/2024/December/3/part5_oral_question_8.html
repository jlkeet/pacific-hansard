Oral Question 8

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you, Mr Speaker, and Kia Orana to everyone in the House today. My question is directed to the Minister of Justice. We will soon be discussing Parliament Paper No. 155, which is an interim report from the Bills, Petitions, and Papers Committee. I understand there was a roadshow that visited New Zealand earlier this year, which we discussed in the House. It was recommended that a report from this roadshow be submitted to Parliament, with a proposed submission date by the end of June this year. I would like to ask the Minister if there is an update regarding this report. During the roadshow, topics such as occupation rights and leases were discussed with our people. I raise this matter because some of the issues addressed with our people in New Zealand and Australia may be relevant to the review conducted by the Bills, Petitions, and Papers Committee.
