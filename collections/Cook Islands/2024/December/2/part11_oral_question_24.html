Oral Question 24

MR V. TUPA: Thank you very much, Mr Speaker. This question is directed to the Minister for Justice. I recently read an article in the newspaper regarding an Act from 1975, which states that only four religious denominations are recognised in the Cook Islands: the CICC, Catholic, LDS, and SDA. These are the only denominations permitted under this Act.
My question is whether you have the capacity to review this Act and potentially limit the introduction of other religions into our country. Our Constitution identifies this nation as a Christian country, which leads me to suggest that we reconsider this Act to maintain a limitation
