Oral Question 3

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you, Mr Speaker, for the opportunity to respond to the question raised by the Honourable Member for Matavera. The inquiry is about the ownership of a machine. However, I am uncertain which machine the Member is referencing, as I have six machines at home, including eight blowers, eight hedge trimmers, eight grass cutters, and two excavators. I believe the machine the Member of Parliament is referring to is the digger.
The digger that I am utilising does not belong to the government, nor is it owned by George Maggie. It belongs to the community and is intended to assist the people in their work without any charge. That is the answer to your question, Honourable Member for Matavera.
