I would like to move a Motion -
Mr Speaker, in light of the comments made by the Chairperson of the PAC Committee, and the fact that they have not yet reached any conclusions regarding their review of these Papers, particularly Paper No. 130 related to the Public Service, which I previously moved for consideration. It is essential to draw comparisons between the reports from the Ministries and the decisions or findings of the PAC. Therefore, I would like to move a Motion:
That all these Papers be deferred until the next sitting of Parliament when the PAC Report on these Papers are completed so we can really draw comparisons between the Papers from the Ministries and the findings of PAC
As you will understand, Mr Speaker, I would also like to address the other Papers to draw comparisons between the reports from the Ministries and the findings of the PAC. It is important to assess the actual work being done and whether we are receiving a return on our investments in these Ministries. This is the main reason behind my Motion to defer all Papers until the next sitting of Parliament. Thank you.

While I support the Motion, I second the Motion, we should be mindful, I'd ask if we be mindful that each report should be relevant. We should not be discussing reports that are two years old. Those are my comments, thank you.
