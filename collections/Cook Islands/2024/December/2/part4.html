OBITUARIES
We now reach the segment of our obituary notices. On behalf of everyone present today, I would like to extend our heartfelt condolences to all the families who have lost loved ones, from the island of Pukapuka to Mangaia and throughout all the islands of the Cook Islands; our deepest sympathies go out to you all.
Before I forget, I want to take a moment to thank our Chaplain for his Message, which serves as a beautiful reminder for us all to love one another.
It is good to see everyone in the Chamber today; let us now get started with our work.
