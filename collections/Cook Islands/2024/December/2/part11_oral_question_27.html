Oral Question 27

HON. T. ELIKANA: I would like to provide a brief supplementary response to the Minister of Justice's comments. As you know, Mr Speaker, Article 64 of our Constitution guarantees fundamental principles and rights. These rights emerged after the 1975 Act was enacted, which is why the law remains valid; it has not been amended and is recognised as valid under Article 64 of the Constitution. If we consider changing or amending that Act, we must ensure compliance with Article 64, which guarantees equality and freedom of religion for everyone in the Cook Islands. This is just a short note to inform the Member from Matavera before any action is taken regarding that Act.
