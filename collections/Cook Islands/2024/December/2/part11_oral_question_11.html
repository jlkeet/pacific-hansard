Oral Question 11

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you, Mr Speaker, and thank you to the Honourable Member for Matavera for your question regarding the Vaka Council. I have been reminded by my government colleagues that it was your democratic party, while in government, that was responsible for abolishing the Vaka Council. As such, we have not given any consideration to the re-establishment of the Vaka Council here in Rarotonga at this time.
At this moment, our primary focus is on supporting the Rarotonga community through various initiatives, such as community policing and programs led by Members of Parliament who are more directly involved with their constituencies. Therefore, to provide a brief answer to your question, we have not given any consideration to the re-establishment of the Vaka Council.
