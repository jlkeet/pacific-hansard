Oral Question 1

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. First, Kia Orana to everyone here this afternoon. I have a question that I would like to direct to the Honourable George Angene, and it may also pertain to Minister Albert Nicholas. However, I would prefer that Minister George Angene respond to my inquiry.
Firstly, I would like to pose a question to Minister Angene concerning a machine being utilised in Tupapa, Matavera, and Ngatangiia. Specifically, I would like to know if the government purchased this machine and whether it is generating revenue through its use.
