I call the Chairperson for the Constitutional Review Special Committee to present the Committee’s report.
HON. T. ELIKANA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. While the Constitutional Review Special Committee made every effort to prepare a final report for this Sitting, it unfortunately proved logistically and technically impossible to do so. I would like to thank the staff of Parliament for their dedicated efforts to accommodate the Committee's request in order to have a final report ready for presentation today.
Mr Speaker, I am now presenting to Parliament the Interim Report, which outlines the challenges we faced in providing a final report. I also wish to thank the Business Committee for passing a resolution that allows additional time for the Constitutional Review Special Committee to continue its important work in the future. I hereby table this report in Parliament.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you, Honourable Minister Elikana.
