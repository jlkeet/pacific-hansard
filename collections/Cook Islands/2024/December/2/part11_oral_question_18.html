Oral Question 18

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to everyone this afternoon. I see we have the news reporter present in our House who covered this topic in the Cook Islands Newspaper. Welcome, madam, to our Parliament. Mr Speaker, I have received questions from the news reporter regarding the establishment of a mosque in our country. I have forwarded those questions to the Secretary of Justice and the Crown Law Office to seek further clarification.
I have informed the Cook Islands News that there are currently three pending applications for new faith denominations to be registered in the Cook Islands. However, concerning the Muslim Mosque, there is no application at this time. I will be happy to share additional information with this House once I receive further clarification from Crown Law. Thank you very much.
