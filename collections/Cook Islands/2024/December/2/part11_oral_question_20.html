Oral Question 20

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you very much, Mr Speaker. After hearing what the Prime Minister has discussed, I would like to make a request on behalf of Vaka Puaikura. We are facing significant challenges in our district, especially as we approach the festive season.
I urge the Prime Minister, who is also the Minister of Police, to make community policing a reality for us in Puaikura. This initiative was very successful during Aporo Miria's tenure. Therefore, I kindly ask for your consideration in allocating a police officer to ensure the protection of our community and tourists. Thank you.
