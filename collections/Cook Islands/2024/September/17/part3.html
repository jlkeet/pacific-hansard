OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): Please take your seats as Parliament is now in session. Kia Orana to everyone present in this House this afternoon. As the Chaplain of the House has already greeted everyone, allow me to extend the same. Kia Orana to our people residing in the Northern and Southern Group islands as well as to those who are watching our livestream from overseas. Kia Orana to each one of you!
Secondly, we would like to express our deepest condolences and offer words of comfort to the families that have lost their loved ones in the recent weeks. We mourn the loss of Mama Mii Temaeu-o-te-rangi Teikamata Ariki, OBE of Nukuroa, Tetika Mataiapo, Mama Katuke Kopa of Tikioki, Papa Nooroa Meti of Turoa, Papa Ben Tamariki, and Papa Nooro Ukere of Avatiu. We also extend our heartfelt condolences to anyone who has lost their loved ones recently. Parliament sympathises with all the families in their time of grief.
