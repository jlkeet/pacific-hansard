Oral Question 1

MR V. TUPA: Mr Speaker, may I seek your permission to direct my question to the Honourable Prime Minister? Before I do so, I kindly request your consent to clarify the context of my question. This request stems from a statement made by the Member of Tupapa during which he announced that he will not be standing in the next election. Consequently, he suggested that it may be appropriate to allocate two seats for the village of Tupapa/Maraerenga.
The rationale behind this proposal is that the Te Au o Tonga constituency has the largest number of registered voters, totaling 1,249 when compared to other Vakas. As a result, it is the largest constituency.
The Te Au o Tonga constituency has a total of over 3,900 registered voters, out of which 1,249 voters belong to the Tupapa/Maraerenga tapere. This makes Tupapa/Maraerenga the largest Tapere on Rarotonga in terms of registered voters. Additionally, the distance from one end of Tupapa/Maraerenga to the other is approximately five kilometres. I hope this background information clarifies the basis of my inquiry. For any further details, please feel free to approach me.
Honourable Prime Minister, if I may, I kindly request a direct response to my inquiry. Is it appropriate to have two Members for Tupapa/Maraerenga constituency?
