I call the Chairperson for the Constitutional Review Special Committee for the Committee’s interim report.
HON. T. ELIKANA: Mr Speaker, I rise to present a verbal report from the Constitutional Review Committee in accordance with Standing Order 153 (6). However, due to the unavailability of fuel in the Northern Group, the Committee has been unable to travel and conduct consultations with the people in that area. I am pleased to inform you that the fuel delivery boat has arrived in Rakahanga or Manihiki and will facilitate the travel of the Committee to the Northern Group soon. The Committee is working diligently to complete the consultation process and prepare the report for presentation in the next sitting of Parliament. Thank you.
