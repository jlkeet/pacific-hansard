COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
I understand that the staff have distributed a form to all Honourable Members this afternoon for the Declaration of Interest. Pursuant to Section 18 (1) of the Civil List Act 2005, every Member and the Speaker shall make an annual declaration disclosing any financial or other interest held by him in any company, partnership or unincorporated body.
Please complete these forms today for Parliament record, and hand them over to the Clerk before you leave here. Thank you, Honourable Members.
