Oral Question 3

HON. V. R. TOKI-BROWN: Thank you, Honourable Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to the Clerk of Parliament and all the staff of Parliament and to all the Honourable Members of the House. Greetings to all our people listening and watching live to this Sitting.
In a short response to the question raised by the Honourable Member from Matavera. Whether a Committee has been appointed in accordance to the legislation for the Ministry of Agriculture that was enacted in 2021?
Yes, a Committee was appointed in accordance with the legislation in 2022 and the Chair of the Committee is Lafala Turepu. I believe that I have answered your question.
