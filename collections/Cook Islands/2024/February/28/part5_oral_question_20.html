Oral Question 20

MR SPEAKER: Honourable Minister, the time is up. I think you can continue with your answers tomorrow. There is also an outstanding question to be answered by the Honourable Minister Tingika Elikana and it was for the question raised by the Honourable Leader of the Opposition, Tina Browne. In that case, I will request the Honourable Minister to respond tomorrow. The Honourable Tingika Elikana is due to arrive Rarotonga later this afternoon.
Again, I would like to bring to your notice Honourable Members, about our Oral Question. We all know what Oral Question is but we go on and on before stating our questions. And then when we respond to the questions raised, we go on and on again with our explanations before answering the questions. Our European ways are different. We ask our questions and then we answer the questions. It is called questions with no explanation. It is called open and closed questions.
Therefore, do not worry when someone exaggerate with his or her questions, you would do the same when you respond. But let me remind those who asks questions, to bear in mind about the time.
We have come to the end of Oral Question, thank you very much Honourable Members.
