Oral Question 14

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I have six questions to ask to the six Ministers of government. I noticed there is one Minister not here. I have three Supplementary Questions to ask to the Associate Ministers of government.
The first question is directed to the Minister of Agriculture because under the Act of the Ministry of Agriculture, this Act was passed in this House in 2021. Under Part 2 of this Act provides for the selection of a committee. Those supposedly to be selected to be on this committee are people knowledgeable on agriculture and it is the responsibility of the Head of the Ministry to see to this. To shorten my explanation Mr Speaker, two weeks ago the growers held a meeting and would like to let you know that over two hundred people came to this meeting. The meeting was chaired by Kiriau Turepu and the Kings Representative. I was sad because of what was said on what is happening with agriculture in our country. So, the question to the Minister is, if there is such a committee appointed since the endorsement of the Act in 2021?
