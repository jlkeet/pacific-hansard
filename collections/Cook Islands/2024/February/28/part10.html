We know that for some years back, we were behind with Audit Reports or Annual Reports filed in Parliament. The reasons for that were many including confusion between what they file with Public Service Commissioner and what they file with Parliament.

I was also fortunate to have been asked to participate in a webinar and this webinar was a forum on zoom where there were a panel of four and people will link in by zoom to participate in the discussions.
