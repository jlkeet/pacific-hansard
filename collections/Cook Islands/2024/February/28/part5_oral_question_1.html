Oral Question 1

MR T. VARU: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to you and all the staff of Parliament. I stand to respond to the question that has been raised by the Honourable Minister and Member for Tupapa/Maraerenga. Before I answer the question, I just want to inform the House that I have visited this place to see what has been asked for me to do. And in my investigation to identify the borders of my constituency, I was not sure whether this particular spot is in the Ruaau area or in the Nikao area. But I would like to praise the Honourable Minister for the question.
In answer to your question Honourable Minister, I do agree that these rusted metal barriers do not look nice. And in these recent days we were able to go and cut these rusted metals and removed them. And there are a few leftovers which you can remodel and make it beautiful. And for the remaining area at Blackrock, we will investigate as to how we can improve it.
