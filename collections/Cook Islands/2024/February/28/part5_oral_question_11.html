Oral Question 11

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Mr Speaker, the reason why I asked this question in the last Sitting is because I wanted to make sure that this Aunt of mine has a vehicle. If she had confirmed me then that she did not have one, I would have went around and fought for her to have a vehicle because she is related to me. That was the intention for my question. So, my Supplementary Question for now is if she wants me to find a replacement for her vehicle?
