Oral Question 5

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker, and my appreciation to the Minister for sharing details about the trip to Hawaii. I have a Supplementary Question addressed to the Leader of the Nation. Based on the assurance I received from the Members of Ngatangiia and Titikaveka, they have volunteered to participate in the upcoming festival in the Cook Islands. I strongly believe that, in 2028, the festival will be held in Noumea. My question is directed to the Honourable Prime Minister to confirm whether it is true that the festival will be held in the Cook Islands in 2032, as two Members from Titikaveka and Ngatangiia are prepared to participate in the festival.
