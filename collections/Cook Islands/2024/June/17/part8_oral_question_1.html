Oral Question 1

MR V. TUPA: Mr Speaker, my question is for the Minister of Cultural Development. He, along with the Prime Minister, led the Cook Islands delegation to Hawaii. Upon reviewing the delegation list, I found my name on it initially, but it turned out to be my son with the same name who was travelling. I would like to inquire about our position regarding the delegations that travelled with the team, including the carvers, and request the Minister of Culture to share information about what took place.
