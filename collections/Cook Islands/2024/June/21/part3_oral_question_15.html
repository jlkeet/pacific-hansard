I see the Honourable Member Vaitoti Tupa; you have the Floor.
MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I will not take much time since I fully support the Internal Affairs allocation. However, I would like to make a brief comment about Rarotonga's beautification program. I am aware of a delay in the contract signing, and the Office of Crown Law is examining this matter.
Additionally, I would like to request that the Minister explore an area of concern regarding the proposed diversion of funds from the beautification scheme to other areas within the Ministry next year. Specifically, a $50,000 appropriation is proposed for 2025/26's allocation to other Ministry projects, which may impact Puaikura and Takitumu areas where work is already underway.
I want to express that Members from Vaka Takitumu would appreciate this allocation being used to beautify our areas and in Puaikura. Thank you to the Ministry's Secretary and the Minister Responsible for their continued support in recognising the need for beautification in our Vaka.
In the past, there was an allocation of $5,000 for each Vaka for stream beautification and maintenance, which is no longer in place. Members of Matavera and Ngatangiia, including myself, have been required to clear these streams independently when they become blocked after rain.
I would like to thank the Minister of ICI for supporting us in the past during flood times. My request is to support this Appropriation for Internal Affairs and to address these concerns. Thank you.
