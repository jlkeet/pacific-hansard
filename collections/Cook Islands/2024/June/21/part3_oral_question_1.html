Oral Question 1

MR SPEAKER: It appears that there are no other bereavement notices to share, and we will now proceed with our work. Moving on to the Question Time, I would like to invite the Honourable Prime Minister to take the Floor.
HON. M. BROWN: Thank you, Mr Speaker. In order to facilitate more speaking time for our Estimates and Appropriation Bill and the consideration of detail of the Vote Items, I request Members to consider Standing Order 49 (5) where the Government Orders of the Day take precedence particularly if the Budget Debate on the consideration of detail for the Appropriation is before the House. This will allow us an additional 30 minutes during the day to debate these vote items. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
