Oral Question 12

HON. A. NICHOLAS: May I ask for my statement not to be translated because I will run out of time.
MR SPEAKER: Request declined.
HON. A. NICHOLAS: I agree with you, Member for Akaoa, that I have no objections to anything you said. The views you expressed regarding taxpayers and the Puaikura industry align with those of the Members for Ngatangiia and Titikaveka, who have also shared similar sentiments with me. However, it would be remiss and inappropriate of me to prioritise fixing roads in Puaikura over those in other regions.
As evident in the 2024/25 financial year budget, there will be a significant reduction in ICI's Capital Budget to align with our fiscal capacity. However, Mr Speaker, the fiscal sacrifices that we will make in the upcoming year will pave the way for substantial increases in ICI's overall capital and maintenance budget in the following years.

I anticipate that in the 2024/25 Budget, Mr Speaker, ICI will continue to concentrate on core capital investments. The sealing of Nikao and the Avatiu back road will be a primary focus, followed by the Titikaveka main drag, and then Arorangi.
Mr Speaker, as the Minister of ICI, I have prioritised delivering our projects in an orderly and systematic manner. We now focus on completing one road from start to finish, rather than following the past culture of randomly working on multiple projects, resulting in unfinished roads.
I acknowledge that financial constraints have resulted in slower progress than desired, but rest assured, my intention is to ensure all our roads are completed. However, Mr Speaker, there are factors beyond my control, just as they are beyond the Prime Minister's control. Work can only be completed as funds become available. Unless some companies in the Cook Islands are willing to contribute by delivering work for free, perhaps a company with which the Honourable Member is involved can tar seal Puaikura at no cost.
I am pleased to collaborate with the Honourable Member's son to develop all the necessary technical designs. I understand that this will require significant financial resources. However, I can assure the Honourable Member that as long as I serve as the Minister of ICI and my government stays in power, I am committed to completing the Puaikura road. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
