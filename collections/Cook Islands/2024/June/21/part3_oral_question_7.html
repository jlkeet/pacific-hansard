Oral Question 7

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Mr Speaker, I think I might only have two messages. The first one is I was not really going to speak on this Vote Item. The reason being is in my country of Rakahanga, we don’t have a policeman. The reason why we don’t have a policeman is because we have no crime. So, when I hear everyone else asking for village police that means there is a lot of crime in their vaka. So, we will continue Prime Minister to have no policeman until we have crime in our country.
My second message is a more serious message is that I have been promoting increase in wages for the Police officers for quite sometimes. But there is a proviso with that Minister and that proviso is that we pay for quality service not just pay for anything. On the other day Prime Minister, you were mentioning the challenges in the Police Department and what is going to be happening with their assistance of their accounts management etc. I think the quality performance coming out in the service provided by our police also needs to be looked at with a magnifying glass. But it is not going to be easy I understand but I think if we are able to put plans in place that meets these challenges then we would be ahead. Thank you.
