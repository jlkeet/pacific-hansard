Oral Question 10

MR T. HERMAN: Thank you Mr Speaker, while this question should have been directed to the Minister responsible for CIIC, I will respond to it. The Aitutaki harbour project falls under their jurisdiction, and it began approximately two years ago. As per the budget for the upcoming financial year, $1.2 million has been allocated for this project. Recently, the CEO for CIIC was summoned by the PAC committee, and the matter of the Aitutaki harbour was discussed during the session. There is considerable public concern about this project, and so we have asked the CEO for CIIC to submit a comprehensive report to PAC regarding its status. Therefore, Honourable Member, we are currently awaiting their report. Once we receive the report, it will be presented in Parliament. From our observations, the project is progressing satisfactorily, but only time will reveal when it will be completed. Thank you.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you. I see the Honourable Member Tetangi Matapo; you have the Floor.
