Oral Question 8

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker, I had intended to address my question to the Minister for Corrective Services. However, I will redirect my inquiry to the Member of Parliament from Aitutaki, specifically for the Vai-Vai-Tau constituency. I understand that this Member was actively involved in the Aitutaki harbour project. During my visit to Aitutaki for the Ureia village Christmas celebration last year, I witnessed him inspecting the project's progress. Therefore, my question for the Honourable Member is about the current status of the harbor project. According to the budget, certain tasks are required to be completed for this project. I would like to know more about it. Thank you.
