Oral Question 4

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to us all in the Name of our Lord. I had planned to pose a question to the Honourable Minister for Corrective Services regarding a new variety of bean seed. However, I will redirect my query to the Minister responsible for CIIC.
Honorable Minister, I apologise in advance for any issues regarding protocol. My question pertains to concerns raised last year regarding the Rutaki foreshore passage.
For several years now, the tides have been causing severe erosion along the main road near Ian Beaton's residence and the Jehovah's Witness Church. Unfortunately, the response from ICI is that there is no budget allocated to address this issue. It is well-known that there are no funds available for this matter.
Thank you to the government for their dedicated efforts in the past years, particularly with the ongoing renovations of the Sheraton Hotel, including the Rutaki School. The results of these projects are truly impressive.
I have one more request, which concerns the Rutaki passage. My question and request are as follows: Is it possible to acquire funding for the area, or alternatively, could you arrange for your staff to visit the location and observe the ongoing damage? Thank you, Honourable Minister.
