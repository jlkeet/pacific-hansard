Oral Question 11

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. My question is directed towards the Member for Titikaveka; however, I currently see him occupied with his mobile phone. Fortunately, I have multiple inquiries to make. Firstly, I would like to address the Minister responsible for sports by inquiring about the current status of our sports individuals. The Olympics in France will commence in approximately two months, and many of our people are concerned about the athletes representing our nation. Can the Minister please provide an update regarding this matter?
