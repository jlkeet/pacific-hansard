Oral Question 4

MR. V. TUPA: Mr Speaker, I would like to comment briefly on the appropriation for BTIB. Firstly, I would like to express my support for their allocation. However, I want to draw attention to their Output 3 - Foreign Investment. I urge the Minister responsible to reconsider this and strive for improvement. It is vital, especially for those in the business sector and foreign investors seeking to enter the country. I believe that we can make Output 3 better if we review it again, and I strongly support this proposal.
