Oral Question 15

MR V. TUPA: Apologies Mr Speaker for interrupting. As the Member for Titikaveka is taking a considerable amount of time, I have decided to ask my third question now and postpone my query to him till tomorrow.
My third question is aimed at the Minister responsible for Corrective Services. I take this moment to compliment his choice of attire today, which resembles that of a correctional facility uniform. It has come to my attention that renovation work is undergoing at our prison, with inquiries being made on the matter. Hence, I would like to ask the Minister about the status of the refurbishment project at the prison, considering the attire that he currently adorns. Thank you.
