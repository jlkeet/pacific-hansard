Oral Question 13

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you, Mr Speaker, and the Member for Matavera for the great question. I would like to take this opportunity to share with all the listeners and viewers that our Cook Islands team is fully prepared to attend the Olympics. If only we had the resources to fund a group of dancers, I would have taken them along, given that in our previous trip to the Solomon Islands, we won a gold medal.
Mr Speaker, it is my pleasure to announce that three athletes have been confirmed to represent our nation. The first of them is Mr Alex Beddoes who will be competing in athletics, while the remaining two are swimmers - Mr Wesley Roberts and Ms Lanihei Connolly - who come from the region of Pokoinu/Nikao, with one of them from Atiu. We take immense pride in the fact that these three individuals will represent our country at the Olympics.
Additionally, I would like to remind those who were unable to participate in this event to be prepared for the upcoming Cook Islands games which will take place in September/October. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
