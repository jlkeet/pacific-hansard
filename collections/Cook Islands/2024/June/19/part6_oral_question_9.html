Oral Question 9

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you to the Honourable Member from Murienua for your question. You are correct that some companies are experiencing a lack of work and, as a result, have laid off employees. Regarding the question of why CIIC has undertaken these projects, it is due to financial considerations, Mr Speaker. During project planning, costings were taken into account. However, after discussing the allocated funding with contractors, it became apparent that the funding was insufficient to complete the project.
To summarise, Mr Speaker, I would like to share a recent example. We had a budget of $60,000 for tarsealing work on the Ports Authority parking area, and we sent out an RFQ. Unfortunately, the lowest quote we received was $200,000. As a result, we are unable to pay the contractor as we only had $60,000 allocated for the project. In short, Mr Speaker, it comes down to costs and what we can or cannot afford. Thank you.
