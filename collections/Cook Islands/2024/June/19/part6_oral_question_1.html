Oral Question 1

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to you and all your staff this morning. Salutations have already been conveyed to us this morning.
I stand to provide a response to the question raised by the Leader of the Opposition yesterday. The question pertained to the complaints that were submitted to the Office of the Ombudsman. I have since received a response from the Ombudswoman.
According to the report, the Office of the Ombudsman received eight complaints from the Police Office between 2017 to 2024. Out of the eight complaints, only two have been resolved, while the remaining six are awaiting a report from the Police Office detailing the actions taken. The delay in the resolution of these complaints is attributed to the extended time taken by the Police to furnish the results of investigations to the Office of the Ombudsman.
The lack of response from the Police to requests for information from the Ombudsman is a significant concern. This issue, unfortunately, is not limited to the Police and is also prevalent in other Ministries within the Government. Although we cannot provide specific details regarding the nature of the complaints, we are willing to address any complaints that have been submitted to us through the Ombudsman's office. I hope my responses have been satisfactory. Thank you.
