COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
Pursuant to the Standing Order 48 (1) (e), I would like to inform the House that on Monday, June 24th at 9 a.m. the launching ceremony for the consolidated laws of the Cook Islands will be held on our Parliament grounds. Invitations and the programme have already begun to be distributed; if you have not yet received yours, it will be sent to you soon and it will be an honour to see you at that function.
Secondly, I have received notice that the Honourable Member Stephen Matapo is still unwell. We wish him a speedy recovery and look forward to seeing him back in the House again.
The Floor is open to Honourable Members. It appears that there are currently none at this time.
