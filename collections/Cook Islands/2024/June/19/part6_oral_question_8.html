Oral Question 8

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I am uncertain if you have already conveyed this, but it is critical to remind Members not to share stories during Question Time as it is counterproductive. With that said, I would like to direct my question to the Minister for CIIC. As we are aware, some contract workers, particularly carpenters, are departing due to the lack of available work. We understand that CIIC is accountable for renovating the MFEM building and Tupapa Outpatient facility. My question to the Honourable Minister is, why were these projects not tendered out to contractors to provide work to these individuals and prevent them from leaving the country, for instance, to Land Holdings Ltd?
