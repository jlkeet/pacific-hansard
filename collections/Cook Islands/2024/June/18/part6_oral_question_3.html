Oral Question 3

MR T. HEATHER: Mr Speaker, I would like to ask a Supplementary Question to the Prime Minister and also Minister for Police. I appreciate the response given by the Honourable Prime Minister, but my concern, as well as the concern of the people, is the current situation. We are faced with a major issue in our country. Thieves are rampant in the Arorangi and town areas, and there has been an increase in accidents. Therefore, I would like to ask the Prime Minister what measures the government plans to take to address this problem. The safety of our people is at risk, not only on the roads but also in their homes and businesses. This problem is escalating, and we need swift action. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
