Oral Question 11

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I would like to direct my question to the Minister for Punanga Nui. It is a crucial matter that needs clarification. As a participant in the meeting of Punanga Nui vendors, there was considerable discussion concerning renovating and upgrading Punanga Nui. I believe the Minister is spearheading this initiative. Therefore, I need to inquire whether the said upgrading work extends to the coastal area of Punanga Nui and the old buildings in its vicinity. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Minister, you have the Floor.
