Oral Question 12

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you very much to the Honourable Member for Matavera for your excellent question. As we both have connections to Manihiki, I will address your inquiry. Thank you, Mr Speaker, for the chance to respond.
There are two Ministers who oversee Punanga Nui. The first is myself, Honourable Member George Angene, and the second is the Deputy Prime Minister, Honourable Albert Nicholas. Nevertheless, I am uncertain if your question is intended for me or the Deputy Prime Minister. Regardless, I will do my best to provide a response.
Regarding the coastal area of the market, I had extensive plans for this region when I was the Minister. Nonetheless, because I did not have control over the finances for this area previously, I could not implement my ideas. If I were the Minister in charge of Punanganui Market's budget, I would have already constructed buildings and an Olympic-sized swimming pool for the children.
Thank you to the Deputy Prime Minister Abert Nicholas for affirming the construction of a large farmers' market complex for the farmers' benefit and utilisation. The current area where the Maroro hut is situated, and fried fish is sold and consumed, and the public toilet will be moved further down to the coast. Additionally, we will construct vending shelters further down the coast and move the playground area to the Tupapa end near the Terevete Park.
Mr Speaker, when the New Zealand Deputy Prime Minister visited, he granted some money for this development project, and the current government has also provided funding for this initiative. Thank you to the Prime Minister and your department for your assistance in progressing this plan. Our target is to conclude the project by the end of the year. That is my response to the Member from Matavera's question.
