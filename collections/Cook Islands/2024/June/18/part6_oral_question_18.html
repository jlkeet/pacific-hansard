Oral Question 18

MR T. VARU: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to all Members of this House. As I have observed that Question Time is running short, I request that you consider extending the allotted time. My inquiry is directed to the Deputy Prime Minister.
Last Saturday, I, along with several Members of Parliament, visited Mangaia for the closing celebration of the 200th anniversary of the arrival of the Gospel. During the event, Mr Taoro Brown donated a sum of money from the Airport Authority to support the occasion. I genuinely believe that this is a significant donation. My question is whether the same donation has been given to other islands such as Aitutaki, Mauke, Mitiaro, and Atiu for a comparable amount as that given to Mangaia. Thank you.
