SUMMONS TO ATTEND HIS EXCELLENCY THE QUEEN’S REPRESENTATIVE
The Clerk, please see who is knocking.
CLERK-ASSISTANT: Mr Speaker, the Queen’s Representative’s Messenger is at the door.
MESSENGER: Mr Speaker, I wish to inform you and this Honourable House that His Excellency the Queen’s Representative desires the attendance of this Honourable House at the National Auditorium Chamber.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Messenger. Honourable Members we shall now proceed to the National Auditorium Chamber.
(Mr Speaker, the Clerk-Assistant, Second Clerk-Assistant and the House proceed to the National Auditorium Chamber)
Sitting suspended at 10.30 a.m.
Sitting resumed at 11.39 a.m.
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Members of the House I wish to Report that I with Members of Parliament did attend upon his Excellency the Queen’s Representative Sir Apenera Short KBE, this morning at the National Auditorium Chamber. And His Excellency was pleased to hand me His Speech which I now lay on the Table.
