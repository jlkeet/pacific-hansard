OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T.TURA): Please be seated. I call the House to order. Kia Orana to all of us this afternoon, Kia Orana in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ.
To the Prime Minister and to your Deputy and all Members of your Cabinet and Government, Kia Orana. To the Leader of the Opposition and all your Members, Kia Orana. To all our family and friends in the Gallery who had joined us this afternoon for our special occasion, we welcome you to this Honourable House.
Our first business of the House today is the swearing-in of our new Member of Parliament. Like I said in Maori, Honourable Members, the first item on our business paper today is the swearing-in of the new Member for Manihiki, the Honourable Akaiti Puna, pursuant to Standing Order 32. May I, on behalf of all Members of Parliament, congratulate Mrs Puna on her successful election to this Honourable House.
I now invite Mrs Puna to rise and present herself at the Table to be sworn in as the new Member for Manihiki.
Mrs Akaiti Puna, by the powers vested in me as Speaker of the House, I hereby invite you to take the Oath of Allegiance as prescribed by Article 30 of the Constitution of the Cook Islands.
(Swearing-in of a new Member of Parliament)
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Members, that completes the swearing-in of our new Member this afternoon. Congratulations Mrs Puna and may you have a joyful and productive journey throughout your term in Parliament.
It is my honour now to say, that Parliament is hereby suspended until 3.00 p.m. to enable the newly sworn Member to spend time with her family, followed by refreshments.
Sitting suspended at 1.12 p.m.
Sitting resumed at 3.00 p.m.
