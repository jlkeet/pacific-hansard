I propose the Question:

I see the Honourable Vaitoti Tupa; you have the Floor.
MR V. TUPA: Thank You, Mr Speaker. I rise to give my support to this Vote Item because when we look at the Appropriation ending 30th June, it has reached $2 million. Mr Speaker, I believe that we know this money comes 30th June, it expires. Then we come to the 1st of July and I believe that is when the 60th Celebration commences ending on 4th August.
What I wanted to know Mr Speaker is whether we have sufficient money for Cultural Development during this celebration period. That is why I stood up Mr Speaker and to support this Vote Item and also awaits how much more finance will be given to Cultural Development for the celebrations.
