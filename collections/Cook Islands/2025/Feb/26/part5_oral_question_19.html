Oral Question 19

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Speaker, and thank you to the Honourable Member for Akaao. In my last meeting with Alatoa, the CEO of Environment Services, which took place about two weeks ago, they were in the process of awarding a contract for that program. Unfortunately, this contract is not for the actual mitigation work or the construction of the necessary structures. Instead, it is for a company to collect data, conduct research, and prepare a proposal for the government.
Currently, Mr Speaker, I share the frustration that many people feel regarding the recent tidal issues in the Takitumu and Puaikura areas. During that time, I was literally on the ground, even though I wasn't a Minister then. I empathised with the people in those areas and felt their anguish. There is a pressing need for this program to advance to the point of taking tangible, physical actions, and I completely understand their concerns.
I assure the Honourable Member that I will follow up with Alatoa to determine the current status of the program.
