Oral Question 3

HON. M. BROWN: Mr Speaker, I think you need to advise the Honourable Member that Oral Question time was at the start of our Sitting. This is the time for Members to exercise their understanding. However, to help the Honourable Member out, the Contingency Fund is a standing fund that is available to Cabinet to use for unanticipated contingencies and usually it is a very modest sum that is made available. In past years not more than a $100 thousand. It is intended for small unanticipated contingencies to give Cabinet some level of flexibility to address an urgent need. Thank you, Mr Speaker.
