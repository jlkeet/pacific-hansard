Oral Question 21

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: My question is to the Minister responsible for the Solar system. Because we are aware that there is a program for the refurbishing of the solar system in the Northern Group. I have asked the Ministry of OPM which is responsible as to when the program is going to start. The reply is that they are not definite and sure of the time.
The reason why I am asking this question because we are running out of funds and the money is being used by the Outer Islands to purchase diesels. So, the sooner for us to get this going in fixing our Solar system then it will alleviate the problem of us buying more diesel.
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Prime Minister; you have the Floor.
