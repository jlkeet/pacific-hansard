Oral Question 4

MR R. HEATHER: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Before I ask my question to the Leader of the country, I would like to share a message with the government; there is a saying that you may have won the battle, but the war still lies ahead. Now, here’s my question for you, Prime Minister, regarding the recent events involving three Chinese warships firing live rounds in international waters off New Zealand and Australia. As of yesterday, they have entered Australia’s exclusive economic zone. Mr Prime Minister, how has our government engaged with Chinese Embassy officials in New Zealand regarding this breach of Australian waters?
