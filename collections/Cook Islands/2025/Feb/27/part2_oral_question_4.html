Oral Question 4

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Mr Speaker, for the opportunity to respond to the question raised by the Member for Murienua. While he has shared a narrative, I appreciate the question. Mr Speaker, this is perhaps one of the saddest moments I have experienced in my political career. It is truly sad to witness a fellow Member of Parliament imply that the government should award every major contract to a single contractor, rather than allowing our own people, whether they are young or old, the chance to participate in these opportunities.
I do not mean to diminish Mr Doherty’s contributions in any way, Mr Speaker. He and his company are indeed very capable, just like the Honorable Member’s company, T&M.
