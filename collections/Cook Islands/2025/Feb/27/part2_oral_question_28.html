Oral Question 28

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: When I was listening to his response, I sensed an implication regarding the source of my information. In the Cook Islands newspaper dated January 4th, the government stated, "The government takes allegations against Cook Islands flagged vessels very seriously." I assume the response you are reading to us is from the Head of the Ministry of Transport.
I am concerned because, meanwhile, the Cook Islands is facing criticism globally due to the sanctioning of eight of its vessels. Yesterday, I heard you, Mr Minister, advising the Member of Parliament for Mangaia on their responsibilities as MPs. I would like to suggest that your role as Minister of Transport includes actively investigating these allegations to determine their seriousness, rather than just relying on your Head of Ministry for Transport.
