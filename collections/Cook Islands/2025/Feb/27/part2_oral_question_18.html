Oral Question 18

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Mr Speaker. I rise to address the Honourable Member’s queries from Oral Question time. So, I would like to ask her, would you prefer a short answer or a long one?
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Member, the short one.
HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Mr Speaker, and thank you to the Honourable Member for Rakahanga. To summarise the response from the Ministry of Transport and Maritime Cook Islands:
1.We are not aware of any Cook Islands-flagged vessels currently under arrest. 2.There are no Cook Islands-flagged vessels involved in the transportation of arms to Russia. 3.Additionally, there are no allegations suggesting that Cook Islands-flagged vessels are
uninsured or posing any environmental risks.
Therefore, we believe that there is currently nothing that requires our attention. That’s the short answer.
