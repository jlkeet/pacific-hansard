Oral Question 12

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Kia Orana to everyone in the House this afternoon. Kia Orana Mr Speaker, the staff of Parliament, the Clerk, the Honourable Prime Minister, Cabinet Members, and fellow Members of the government. Kia Orana to my colleagues in the Democratic Party, the United Party, and everyone present here today. Kia Orana to our people listening to our Sitting on the radio and those watching online.
I will not talk too long, as I have studied our Standing Orders regarding the process of asking and answering questions during Oral Question time. My question to the Prime Minister pertains to the announcement you made about the agreement that was signed between your delegation and representatives from China, which is expected to be made available once the delegation returns. I checked the MFAI webpage, and I found only two agreements listed. One is the overarching agreement, but I am uncertain if this is the correct document because there is no signature on it. The other is the memorandum of understanding on the blue economy, which also appears to be unsigned.
My question to the Honourable Prime Minister is whether you can provide this House with copies of all the agreements signed between us and China. As of now, I am uncertain about the total number of memorandums of understanding that have been established with China, whether there is one, two, three, or more. Therefore, my question is straightforward. Can you confirm, yes or no, if you are able to provide us with copies of these signed agreements with China?
