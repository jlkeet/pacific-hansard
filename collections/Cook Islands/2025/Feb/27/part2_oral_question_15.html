Oral Question 15

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you, very much. Our Oral Question time is heading the right direction. My second question, Honourable Prime Minister, I went on the Seabed Mining website because after I read the Memorandum of Understanding on the Blue Economy, there are three companies that have been given exploration licenses; the CIC Limited, COBALT Seabed Resources Limited and the Moana Minerals Limited.
When I read the Memorandum of Understanding with the Seabed Mining, in my understanding, it appears the government is pursuing the spirit of accepting China to assist us with our Seabed Mining. And the question is, whether the government will interfere with China to assist and support us through buying shares in the company of the Cook Islands.
