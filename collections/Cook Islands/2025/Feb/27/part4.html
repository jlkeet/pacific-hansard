CONSIDERATION OF PAPERS
Before we adjourned yesterday, we were on the Papers for Consideration. Honourable Members, let me reiterate my statement in the House before we adjourned. On 17 September 2024, the Chairperson and the Deputy Chairperson of the Public Accounts Committee set forth a Motion to receive certain Papers for consideration enabling their discussion within Parliament. The Chairperson and the Deputy Chairperson for Public Accounts Committee have indicated that they will not discuss their Papers. However, in order for them to be removed from the Order Paper, we must follow the standard procedure. These Papers will be called and if there are no Speakers, it will be removed from the Order Paper.
