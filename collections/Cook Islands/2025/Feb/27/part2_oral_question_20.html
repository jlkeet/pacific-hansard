Oral Question 20

MRS T. MATAPO: Kia Orana, Mr Speaker, and Members of this House. Kia Orana to our interpreter and thank you for the excellent work that you are doing. My question is directed to the Minister of Education.
Does the Ministry of Education have a plan in place to protect our Cook Islands language, and what measures are being considered to help preserve it? I am asking this question because it was highlighted during a meeting on our island, where it was noted that today, our children are speaking English instead of Cook Island Maori.
