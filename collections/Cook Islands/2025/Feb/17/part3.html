OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): Please be seated. I call the House to Order. Firstly, Kia Orana to everyone in this House. Kia Orana to the Honourable Prime Minister, the leader of our nation, and your Deputy, as well as to all Cabinet Ministers and Honourable Members of the Government, Kia Orana to you all.
Kia Orana to the Leader of the Opposition, Honourable Tina Pupuke Browne, and all your colleagues, Kia Orana to you all. Kia Orana to the Leader of the United Party and all your members. Lastly, Kia Orana to the independent Member of the One Cook Islands Party, Honourable Toanui Isamaela.
I hope you have enjoyed your time with your constituents during the Christmas and New Year holiday. I believe that our parliamentary sessions this year will be fruitful and will bring benefits to our people.
Kia Orana to our people in the Northern and Southern Group islands, as well as to everyone residing here in Rarotonga. Similarly, Kia Orana to our everyone living abroad in New Zealand, Australia, and other parts of the world who are listening to our parliamentary discussions.
Kia Orana to the Secretary of the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management and your staff who are present in the Public Gallery; Kia Orana to you all.
With that, Parliamentary Sitting is now open. I have no announcement for the House and no obituaries to announce, and I am confident that this year will be both productive and beneficial for us.
