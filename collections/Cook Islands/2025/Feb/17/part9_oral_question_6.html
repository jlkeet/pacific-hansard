Oral Question 6

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to everyone this afternoon. Members on the other side of the House, Members on this side of the House, staff members of Parliament, everyone here within this Honourable House and those outside, our friends in the Public Gallery area; Kia Orana.
This morning, we all knew that there was a protest and I would like to thank this Honourable House for accepting our request and for receiving our people who had marched to the front of the Parliament building. When the protest march concluded the media reporters from Rarotonga and also from New Zealand and they were asking individual persons what their opinions are. The person who came to ask me a question and this was his question, why do you think the Prime Minister and the Deputy Prime Minister did not come to Parliament? The only Parliamentarians who appeared was Minister Vaine Mokoroa, Minister Tingika Elikana and the Member of Parliament Sonny Williams. My reply to him was, that he should go and ask them personally because I don’t know why they did not appear.
