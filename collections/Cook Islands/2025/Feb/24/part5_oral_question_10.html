Oral Question 10

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Honourable Speaker. My question is to the Honourable Prime Minister of our country. I would like to inquire about our Sovereign Wealth Fund. This matter has previously been presented to the people of Rarotonga during a public consultation. I am asking this question because I strongly support the Fund.
The question is, since the public consultation, where do we stand with this Fund?
