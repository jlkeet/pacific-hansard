Oral Question 8

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you, Honourable Speaker. Kia Orana to you, Mr Speaker, to the Clerk of this House, to all you staff and to everyone in this House this afternoon.
Honourable Speaker, I request that this House allow me some time to investigate this matter further. I will be reaching out to Mangaia to seek clarification on the situation. Specifically, I will contact the new EO, Mia Teaurima, to inquire about the condition of the airport in Mangaia, as its management falls under the administration of the island government.
ICI only becomes involved when there is a request for assistance from Rarotonga. I believe the airport in Mangaia is in need of a roller to stabilise the runway. I have heard that the roller machine on the island was set on fire by locals. However, Mia Teaurima confirmed to me a couple of weeks ago that he has reached out to a contractor here in Rarotonga to request a roller to help complete the necessary work on the airstrip in Mangaia.
In relation to the question about the airport, please bear with me, I will contact Mia in Mangaia and will consult with the ICI office here in Rarotonga to determine what support we can provide for the airport in Mangaia. Thank you, Honourable Speaker.
