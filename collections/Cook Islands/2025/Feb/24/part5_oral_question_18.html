Oral Question 18

HON. T. PUPUKE-BROWNE: I do not think I got an answer from the Prime Minister. The question was whether there are plans to nationalise shipping in the Cook Islands. The reason why I asked this question is because that term was used in the article and that word means the process of taking privately controlled companies, industries or assets and placing them under government control. And what that means to me is that you will be buying the Cook Islands General Transport and Taio Shipping.
