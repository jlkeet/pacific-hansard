Oral Question 14

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you, Mr Speaker, and to everyone in this House, Kia Orana to you all. Kia Orana to all our people listening on the radio and watching the live stream. Additionally, Kia Orana to those seated in the Public Gallery.
I have a question for the Honourable Prime Minister. In the Cook Islands newspaper, on Friday the 21st you were recorded saying that, government has been working to strengthen domestic shipping through international partnership as the Cook Islands may be the only Pacific nation that does not have a nationalise shipping service. My question, Mr Prime Minister is are you moving to nationalise shipping in our country?
