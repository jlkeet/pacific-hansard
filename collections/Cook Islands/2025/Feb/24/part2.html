MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): I call the House to order. Kia Orana to everyone here today. Honourable Prime Minister, Cabinet Ministers, and Members of Parliament within the Government, Kia Orana. Kia Orana to the Opposition Members, your Honourable Leader, and your colleagues. To the leader of the United Party and your two Members of Parliament, Kia Orana to you all. And a special Kia Orana to our friend Toanui Isamaela from the One Cook Islands Party.
Kia Orana to all our visitors in the Public Gallery; Kia Orana to each of you in the name of the Lord.
Furthermore, let us remember our people from the Northern Group and Southern Group islands, including Rarotonga, Kia Orana to all of them. Additionally, to everyone watching our livestream from New Zealand, Australia, and around the world, Kia Orana to you all.
This week has been very good, as there are no obituary notices to report. In saying that, we will proceed with our business for the day.
