Oral Question 6

MRS T. MATAPO: Kia Orana Honourable Speaker and Kia Orana to us all. I would like to ask a question to the Minister of Infrastructure Cook Islands (ICI). As we are all aware, the condition of the airport on the island of Mangaia has been a concern. Last Friday the flight to Mangaia was cancelled and people both in Mangaia and Rarotonga was waiting for the flight to Mangaia.
The situation concerning our airport in Mangaia is quite concerning. When heavy rain falls on the island, the airport conditions deteriorate, leading to water pooling on the airstrip, which prevents planes from landing. Therefore, I would like to ask the Honourable Minister of ICI what plans are in place to address the issues at the Mangaia airport?
