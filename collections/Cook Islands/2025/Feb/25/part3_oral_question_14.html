Oral Question 14

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Mr Speaker, those are serious allegations being raised from across the Floor. If these allegations are found to be true, then it is essential that a thorough investigation is conducted regarding these issues. I want to be cautious in making any further comments beyond what I have already stated. However, I kindly request the Honourable Member to allow me some time to conduct my own investigation with our team at the Ministry of Transport to gather information to verify what the Honourable Member has suggested. I assure you that if there is any indication of corruption related to the claims shared by the Honourable Member, whether in this House or with the public at large, it constitutes a significant breach of the conditions for being classified as a Cook Islands registered vessel, in my view, Mr Speaker.
If the Honourable Member just give me a day or so, then I will return with some accurate information.
