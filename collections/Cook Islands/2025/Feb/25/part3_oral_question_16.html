Oral Question 16

MR V. TUPA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. My question is directed to the Minister of Education. A news article published in the Cook Islands News on 14 January 2025, stated, “Cook Islands grabbles with the declining education value.” This was a statement made by the Secretary of the Ministry of Education. In this article, the Secretary of Education also stated that the condition of our schools is deteriorating and noted a decline in the passing rates of students taking external examinations.
The article also mentioned that there are currently fewer teachers in our schools. The Secretary of the Ministry of Education indicated that one of the reasons for this decline in the number of teachers is that some have travelled to New Zealand for further studies.
