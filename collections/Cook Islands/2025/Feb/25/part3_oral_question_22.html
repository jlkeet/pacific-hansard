Oral Question 22

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you, Mr Speaker. Thank you to the Member for the questions. Firstly, on the ship from China, I am not sure when this vessel will arrive. I will however ask the Ministry of Finance and Economic Management on the arrangements. Secondly, on the ship from Japan, what I know about this boat is that it is less than 10 years old, it is double propelled and it is ideal to service the needs of the Pa Enua. Right now, the boat is undergoing modifications to expand the cargo holds. The cost of these modifications has been generously covered by the Government of Japan.
