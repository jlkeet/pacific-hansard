Oral Question 20

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you very much, Mr Speaker. I would like to request that the Deputy Prime Minister refrain from lecturing us, as we are well aware of our laws and responsibilities as Members of Parliament.
I have a question for the Honourable Prime Minister regarding shipping, which combines two inquiries. First, it has been confirmed that we are to contribute $3 million, matched by $3 million from China. How long can we expect to wait before receiving this ship? Secondly, regarding the ship from Japan, can you clarify whether this is a gift or another arrangement? Can you provide an explanation?
