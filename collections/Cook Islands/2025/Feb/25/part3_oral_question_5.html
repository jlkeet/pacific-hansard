Oral Question 5

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Under the Standing Orders, Mr Speaker, every Member of Parliament in the Cook Islands is entitled to inquire and request information that is readily available at Ministries and Agencies. I assure you, Mr Speaker, that if the Honourable Member had reached out to ICI last week or spoken with Mia, Mia would have provided her with the same information I am sharing today. Therefore, I want to encourage all Members of Parliament, it is not necessary to come to Parliament to ask these questions, because if we had not sat today, you would not have received an answer.
In closing, Mr Speaker, we have agencies dedicated to serving the welfare of our people and our country. As elected Members of Parliament, you have the right to request any relevant information pertaining to your inquiries.
