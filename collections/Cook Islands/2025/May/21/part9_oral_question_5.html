My next question pertains to Foreign Affairs, and I hope the Minister responsible for this Ministry can address it. I would like to ask the Minister of Foreign Affairs, on how many foreigners are currently in the Cook Islands? The reason for my inquiry is that this matter has been brought up in this House, and our people are still seeking clarity. We have been directed to the Statistics Office, which claims that there are 1,500 foreign workers in our country, but I find that figure hard to believe.
I would like to ask the Honourable Minister for Foreign Affairs if he could provide us with an accurate figure regarding the number of foreign workers residing in the Cook Islands.
