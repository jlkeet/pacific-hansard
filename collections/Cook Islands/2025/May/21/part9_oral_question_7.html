Oral Question 7

HON. T. ELIKANA: Thank you, Honourable Speaker. I would like to thank the Honourable Member for Murienua for this ideal question. My short answer for now is to request the opportunity to gather more information, and I should be able to respond to your inquiry by tomorrow. I will provide further details regarding the numbers and specific foreigners residing in our country. It is important to note that we currently have many tourists in the Cook Islands, who are also considered foreigners.
I may need to spend some time with the Honourable Member to clarify what he is specifically inquiring about regarding foreigners, whether he is referring to foreign workers or all foreign visitors, including tourists and others.
