Of concern however, Mr Speaker, it is the way the matter was reported in the newspaper. Particularly, when it was inferred that it was a robbery and right through the newspaper it interchanges with burglaries. If I refer to the opening statement, “a Kiwi family who arrived on Rarotonga for holiday were robbed of their cash, passports and other items.” Robbery, Mr Speaker, implies some form of violence but when you read the whole story, there was no violence presented against the families or the visitors involved. I think, Mr Speaker, those who reports these issues need to do so in an accurate manner.
