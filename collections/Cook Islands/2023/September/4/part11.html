Considerations of Reports of Parliamentary Committee, Standing Orders 156. I call the Chairman of the Public Accounts Committee, the Honourable Tingika Elikana on the Floor.
MR T. ELIKANA: Mr Speaker, in accordance with Standings Orders 156 (1), I move:
That Parliament take note of the following Papers: Paper No. 35 – Public Accounts Committee Report on the review of the PERC Report into the complaint regarding integrity of information produced by Revenue Management Division; and, Paper No. 36 – Public Accounts Committee Report on the review of the PERC Report on the complaint received regarding Revenue Management Division at Ministry of Finance and Economic Management, MFEM-PAC 18/03

Mr Speaker, the reason for noting those Papers is that we are still awaiting and we have been assured that there will be responses from the Revenue Management Division with regard to those two Reports. So, we are seeking that they be noted and also for any debate on it adjourned until the PAC Committee receives a response from the RMD before we consider it completely. Thank you Mr Speaker.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Tingika Elikana. I call for a Seconder to the Motion?
HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Mr Speaker, I second the Motion on the understanding that when the responses are provided and received by the Public Accounts Committee, we will then have an opportunity to debate it.
Seconded by the Honourable Leader of the Opposition, Tina Pupuke Browne
MR SPEAKER: The Question is:
That the Motion be agreed to?
Motion agreed to
Honourable Members, we have come to the end of Parliament and before we adjourn or after we adjourn, I would like to invite you all for lunch. Parliament is now adjourned until Wednesday, 13th December 2023 at 1.00 p.m.
