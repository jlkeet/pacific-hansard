Oral Question 9

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you Mr Speaker. I have a question for the Minister of Agriculture. I have two questions for the Prime Minister but because he is not present then I will wait for the next Sitting of Parliament. This is a question to the Honourable Minister from the Members of Puaikura. This is more a request from all the three Constituencies of Puaikura to you Honourable Minister, if you may please provide us a tractor. I see that there are three tractors at the Ministry of Agriculture and implements to be used by the tractors are limited.
We, the people of Puaikura, would like to request you Minister for a tractor. This request is from a hard-working individual, Mr Nat Unukua, if we can have a tractor for Puaikura to use.
