ORDERS OF THE DAY
Land (Facilitation of Dealings) Amendment Bill 2023 Second Reading.
I call the Honourable Tina Browne for the Second Reading of the Bill.
HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker. With your indulgence, Mr Speaker and before I move for the Second Reading of the Bill, I would like to briefly explain that the Bill aims to align the original Act with the present-day definition of “owners”as set out in the Member’s Bill to amend the Cook Islands Act 1915. This Amendment ensures congruency and reduces any legal ambiguities between these Acts, notably the Bill includes a retrospective clause, meaning all past decisions under the Act will be considered using the new definitions, ensuring uniformity and legal accuracy.
With those said Mr Speaker, I move:
That the Land (Facilitation of Dealings) Amendment Bill 2023 be now read a Second time
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Tina Browne. I call for a Seconder to the Motion?
MR T. ELIKANA: Mr Speaker, before I offer my support for the Motion, if I could just offer my support for the Bill in its current form and as the Honourable Member for Rakahanga has alluded to, it goes hand in hand with the previous one that we have sent to the Bills Committee which is the Amendment to the Cook Islands Act 1915. I understand there will be a further Motion for the same Bill to be referred to that Committee for it to be taken into public consultation. On those basis, Mr Speaker I second the Motion for a Second Reading of this Bill. Thank you.
Seconded by the Honourable Tingika Elikana
