Oral Question 11

HON. V. R. TOKI-BROWN: Kia Orana to you Honourable Speaker and to all the staff of Parliament and greetings also to every Member of Parliament present here today. I extend a very warm welcome to our people who are listening and watching to our Sitting today, greetings to you all.
To the Honourable Member of Murienua, the Leader of the Cook Islands United Party and Representative from Puaikura. It is really hard for me to respond to the request that you have put forward because I still have the request from the Honourable Member for Matavera and other Members of Parliament also. And I have not been able to provide what has been requested by the other constituencies. Can I just ask for you to wait as we in Agriculture try to investigate?
As has been requested by other constituencies even Nga-Pu-Toru who have requested a tractor for them, we will try our best and see as to how we can satisfy the needs of our people. Thank you.
