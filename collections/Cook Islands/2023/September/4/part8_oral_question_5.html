Oral Question 5

MR R. HEATHER: Thank you and greetings to you Mr Speaker and staff of this Parliament, Cabinet Ministers and all Members of this House, greetings to you all this afternoon. I have a short question for the Minister of CIIC.
In one of our past Sittings, I raised a problem concerning Arorangi School. I believe an allocation have been made to renovate two classrooms for Arorangi School. Since 2020 and up to this time, no work has been done on these two classrooms. We have no Parliament Sitting tomorrow so maybe you can answer my question today. What is the progress? Will any work be done this year or not?
