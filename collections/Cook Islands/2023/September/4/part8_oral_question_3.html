Oral Question 3

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you Mr Speaker. Before I answer the question, I would to say, Kia Orana to you Honourable Speaker, Members of Parliament, staff of Parliament and our people listening in.
I was going to stand up and tell the Honourable Member that for his question I will respond to it tomorrow. But I am reminded by my colleague sitting next to me that we will not be sitting tomorrow, so I will try and answer your question now.
Mr Speaker, this road development work that has been carried out in front of the school, I would like to remind this House that the planning and the construction of this road development was through the process of meeting with the PTA and the principal of the Matavera School. During the period of consultation in regards to this matter, I believe that all the members of the PTA of the Matavera School and all the teachers of the school agreed upon it. But I would also like to remind this House that this road development was started before I came into this Ministerial Office.

The reason for the delay of this development work because the contractor was not prepared and ready to carry out the work. It seems to me that the Member for Matavera is asking whether there will be a speed bump built on the road in front of the school. I would like to inform and confirm with him that the speed bump was not in the proposed road development program agreed and supported by the PTA and teachers of the Matavera School. If we need to install a speed bump on this section of the road, maybe I ask that you investigate whether there will be a support by your village of the speed bump.
I am aware that there are many people who have laid their complaints in regards to this road work. What I have discovered that those people who are complaining, they do not have any background on road engineering work. One of these people is a Protocol Officer, another is an Environmentalist and these are Office Workers not used to open air service. Another person makes a living doing peopleâ€™s lawn mowing. I am sure that was has been planned for this road development and was done by qualified road engineers. What has been designed for this development was not by the Prime Minister and I or the Minister before me; we did not do any of the planning. It was designed by these qualified road engineers before it was taken to the PTA and the consultations with the Principal of Matavera School, before an agreement was made.
Mr Speaker, we need to understand that this has not been completed. I believe, most of the marking will be completed by this afternoon in order to get a clear understanding how the bus depot will operate.
A question has been brought to my attention to why buses do not enter and exit the school grounds anymore, however, I believe that ICI had met with the Bus Company on this very topic which they have declined because it was affecting their timetable. We need to understand that this bus is not a School Bus, it is a public Bus. Therefore, this is the reason why this program came about.
Mr Speaker, if there is a problem with the PTA and the School Principal regarding this program, ICI can easily terminate this project. However, it was the PTA who proposed this and not the community members, and not because of these new Members of Parliament.
I hope this is sufficient in response to your question Honourable Member. Thank you.
