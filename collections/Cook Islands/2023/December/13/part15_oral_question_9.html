Oral Question 9

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to you and your staff and those who are in this House. Also, extend the greetings to our people who are listening in and watching as well.
I want to thank the Honourable Member from Akaoa for asking this question just before we went for lunch. So, during the break, I tried to contact these two agencies that are responsible to me and there was no reply. Mr Speaker, I wanted to dig deep into the question that was asked by the Honourable Member because I agree, he is right in that this job was done inappropriately. Mr Speaker, when you look at our roads today there are works that still needs to be done to beautify our roads. Government is making a huge investment in our roads. Regardless of that, I agree it is not appropriate at what these departments or agencies and SOE’s are doing to our new sealed roads.
Briefly Mr Speaker, I would like to get to the bottom of this concern. Just so we are aware Mr Speaker, it is ICI’s policy that when you damage or do any work on our roads, you must return it back to its original condition. So, Honourable Speaker and the Honourable Member who asked the question, if I can be given some time to come back tomorrow and I will have a response to the question and for our people to hear. I will come back tomorrow and let you know the policy that ICI now follows on the harmful effects to our roads. Thank you.
