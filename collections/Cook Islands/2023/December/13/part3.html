OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): Parliament is now is Session. Kia Orana to everyone in the Name of our Lord. As addressed by our Chaplain in his greeting for everyone this afternoon, I will not repeat it. However, I would like to convey our regards to all our people listening in to our Sitting from the Northern and Southern Group Islands. To our people here in Rarotonga and those living abroad, Kia Orana to you all. I can see that we are going to be very busy in this Sitting; therefore, we will begin with our Sitting.
