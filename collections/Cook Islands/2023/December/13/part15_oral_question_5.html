Oral Question 5

MR R. HEATHER: Thank you Mr Speaker. Firstly, Kia Orana to the Prime Minister and everyone in this House.
My question, Mr Speaker is to the Minister for the Cook Islands Investment Corporation and this is in regards to the Te Aponga Uira and the To Tatou Vai. I would like to shorten my question because these issues have been brought to my attention by our people.
The issue here is the digging of the trenches for electricity. We know in the past that these cables and pipes are laid all at once; the electricity, the water pipes and the telephone lines. The problem we see today is there are two digging of trenches and the one for the Te Aponga Uira electricity lines and the pipes for To Tatou Vai. This is beneficial to the businesses responsible for these kinds of jobs but not for our people.
Since our people have to pay twice to the To Tatou Vai and as well to the Te Aponga Uira. Therefore, my question to the Minister Responsible for CIIC is, whether you are aware of what is happening or whether there is a new policy for this kind of projects.
