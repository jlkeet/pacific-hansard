Oral Question 13

HON. T. HEATHER: Thank you very much Mr Speaker. Also thank you for the clarification in the response from the Honourable Minister. Before the second question, the principles and merits on this has already been explained by the Honourable Prime Minister. The reason Mr Speaker is that the explanation is clear to our people. As he had stated, the person who wanted this service should pay for everything but once you switch the light on is when you start paying for it. The intention in the request is if government through Te Aponga Uira has collected this money during all these times, then why can they not reduce some of the costs? Not with T&M, if T&M is not paid, then there is tax for us to claim in the country.
The second question Honourable Member is on the latest digging about two weeks ago on the main road in front of the dental at the Tupapa Outpatient. The question is why was this dug up again? It has been less than two years. The problem is, how much more money are the taxpayers of this country going to fork out to beautify this road again and yet it has already been beautified. Thank you.
