Oral Question 10

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to all of us and our people listening to our parliament sitting.
I want to make a short explanation and pose my question to the Honourable Minister Albert Nicholas. This is regards to our electricity and you have been in this work for a long time. The Minister mentioned the lowering of the cost on our people and this is what came to my mind to ask my question.
As we know Mr Speaker, the cost to lay a power line from the power pole to any home regardless of the distance, it is the homeowner that bears the cost and we can see that today. Mr Speaker, this is a big burden that our people are taking. The reason is they have to buy the meter box probably supplied by CITC today and store it away at home to keep it dry. Maybe in the future Mr Speaker if government can look into this and subsidise the cost faced by our people. I know the people are suffering because my company is involved in digging the trenches and laying of the cables. So, the question is if the government can assist.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you. Honourable Member, you have the Floor?
