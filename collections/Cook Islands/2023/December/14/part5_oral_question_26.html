Oral Question 26

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker. Yesterday, I asked a question on the charter of the boat to Pukapuka because what I understand is that charter was subsidized by government to the tune of $637,000. When the Member for Pukapuka/Nassau gave his answer, he was explaining how long it took to arrange the charter.
So this is the question, there are three questions, if it is possible to confirm that this charter is subsidized by the taxpayers money. Secondly, if the government have a policy, because we have not seen this policy that holidays in the Pa Enua will be subsidized and three, if the answer is yes there is a policy then can we have a copy or share to us how we can access that assistance. Thank you.
