Oral Question 7

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question goes to the Honourable Prime Minister. I read the newspaper article for Monday 13thMarch 2023 and this article stated, “Police and Customs need to work together.” Before I ask my question to our Prime Minister, I can see our Solicitor General in the House and this question may apply to him.
Mr Speaker, I have been asking these two Government Departments of what the conflict is? They told me that they have their own Legislation which they use to monitor visitors to our country. Police also have their own as well especially with the importation of Marijuana for the use of medicinal purposes. Therefore, the question that I want to ask the Leader ofour Country is a simple question. So, my first question is whether Customs and Police acting on the advice of Crown Law has met to discuss and settle the issues between those two departments on the importation of medicinal marijuana into the country?
