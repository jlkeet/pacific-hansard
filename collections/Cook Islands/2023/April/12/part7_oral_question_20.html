Oral Question 20

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you for your answer Honourable Minister. Mr Speaker, his response is quite diluted too. I have a question for the Prime Minister, Mr Speaker.
It has come to the attention of this country through the Cook Islands Newspaper about the term Sabbatical Leave. Honourable Prime Minister, my question is, have this been resolved? Has the Head for IRD taken this leave with pay for six months? Because my concern is, under our law of the country, for any holiday period, two weeks is ample. But this one, for six months Prime Minister; and with the Salary that he collects!
I was reading some of the comments made by the Head of MFEM regarding one of the reasons for this Leave is because of the hard work that this gentleman has done during COVID-19.
There were many volunteers who did not get paid, who gave hours of support as commented by our Prime Minister, which is rightly so.
So, Honourable Prime Minister, has this been resolved? Is this the actual truth of this matter? Thank you.
