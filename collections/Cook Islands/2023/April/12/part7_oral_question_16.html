I can vouch Mr Speaker in this Honourable House that this machine has been around the entire island, being used. I can show some photos as proof of this particular machinery being used on some properties and that is my concern Mr Speaker, the fact how this machinery was bought by Government funds used by the Ministry of Corrective Services, which the Minister took advantage of. That is the end of my question Mr Speaker.
