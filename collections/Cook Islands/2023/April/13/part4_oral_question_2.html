Oral Question 2

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you Mr Speaker and thank you Honourable Member for the question.
You are asking me how much this machine cost. My response Honourable Member is, I do not know how much it cost because I am not a Finance Officer. Although I cannot count money in digital sense, however, I can count numbers of people who voted for me during Election. I would know exactly the total numbers of voters who voted for me.
In response to your question, I am not sure how much the machinery cost. Thank you.
