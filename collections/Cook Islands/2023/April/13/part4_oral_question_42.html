Oral Question 42

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you to the Member from Matavera. I thought you were going to stand and ask for Matavera roadwork to be done outside of the programme.
But to answer the Honourable Members’ question, plainly Mr Speaker because of the constraints of the annual budget Mr Speaker, which is $800,000 annually. On this request of yours Honourable Member, this means this budget allocation will be spent on your roads alone.
That means the Honourable Sonny Williams and Honourable Vaine Mokoroa will have to wait for another year or onwards for their Constituency road upgrade to be attended to. Hence, the reason why the allocation of the work that has been spread out from one location to another, to spread everyone’s’ share.
The question you raised Honourable Member is a good question. If only the allocation of our budget annually is over $5 million, then I believe the request that you have made for the completion of the work on our roads will be certified under that program.
Consequently, because of the allocation of the budget that has been given for this program, we therefore had to allocate certain work and budget to benefit and shared to every one of our community.
That is the answer and the reason of the allocation and I hope that I have answered your question. So, the answer being, no.
