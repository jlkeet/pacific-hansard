Oral Question 15

MR T. HEATHER: The question Mr Speaker is to our Prime Minister. If this is the case, then a log book should be provided to the Government to show where and when the machine was used. And if that can be made available and be presented to this House or to me personally, then I will support the claims of the work that this machinery was purchased for. It is for the community and this is why I have not seen this machinery at our Prison in Arorangi. Thank you Prime Minister.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member and I see the Honourable Prime Minister; you have the Floor.
HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker. It is not the business of the Members of this House or the business even of Ministers to present working log books of operating machines before Parliament.
If anybody is interested in how much a vehicle is being utilized, the appropriate people to go and see is the heads of those ministries and departments. If the Honourable Member is keen to know the log book and operating records of this particular machine. I would suggest and recommends that he meets with the head of the Corrective Services to be able to discuss those management matters.
Thank you Mr Speaker.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Prime Minister. I see the Honourable Member Teariki Heather.
