Oral Question 20

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you Mr Speaker. First of all, Kia Orana to all Honourable Members in this House. I would like to thank the Honourable Member for Matavera for raising this question on a newspaper article written on 18 March 2023.
As articulated by the Member, the delay of this case with this person being convicted. His question is, if this case has been resolved by Judge Toogood. The answer is, yes and this case been dismissed. I do not think the problem lies with the Ministry of Justice, I think it lies with the Police, as they were the ones who lodged this case to Court.
Therefore, I would recommend for the Member of Matavera to direct his question to the Minister of Police.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Minister Mokoroa. I see the Honourable Leader of the Opposition; you have the Floor.
