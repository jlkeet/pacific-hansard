Oral Question 8

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you Prime Minister for your reply. The reason for my question to the Prime Minister is because of the appropriation in the budget, the allocation is for the people of this country, in this particular ministry.
The Prime Minister is opening a new initiative for all Honourable Members and individual Constituencies, under this new initiative, we can request for ourselves an excavator to help our people.
HON. G. ANGENE: Point of Order!
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Minister, what is your Point of Order?
HON. G. ANGENE: Irrelevance! Mr Speaker, I entered Politics in 2010. In 2011, a meeting was held and the Minister at the time was Teariki Heather. The Members of Parliament were invited, not Ministers, Members of Parliament. At that time, the Honourable Minister Vaine Mokoroa was the Head of Ministry for ICI. We the Members of Parliament at the time was invited to this meeting.
During that time one of the current Ministers of the Crown, Mac Mokoroa was the Secretary of ICI at the time. I requested for a machine to be purchased for all Honourable Members.
