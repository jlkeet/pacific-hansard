Oral Question 18

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is to the Minister for the Ministry of Justice. I believe that he is able to answer my question for this is mentioned in our local newspaper.
It was quoted in the Newspaper article on 18thMarch 2023 that Judge Toogood “slams Criminal Justice System.”
He said that he is a bit worried about the criminal justice system. Mr Speaker, when we read this article, the lengthy period it took for the conviction of this person, I would say Mr Speaker that it took a one year period for this case to be considered.
When I contacted the Ministry of Justice, I asked them who was at fault. They explained it to them that there are many. I was not sure whether the Minister Responsible was at fault or not.
Honourable Minister, the question is, has this case been resolved? The frustration that Judge Toogood experienced, has it been resolved? This case took so long and I wanted to ask if this has been resolved, to prevent Judges from issuing their verdicts. Thank you Mr Speaker.
