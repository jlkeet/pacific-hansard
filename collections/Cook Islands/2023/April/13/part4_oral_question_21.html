Oral Question 21

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Kia Orana everyone. Mr Speaker and your staff, Honourable Prime Minister and your side, our side, Kia Orana. Our guests in the Public Gallery, the Solicitor General and your Assistance, our Media, Kia Orana. Staff of this Parliamentary House who is not in this Chamber, Kia Orana to you all.
I have a question to the Minister of Infrastructure. In the Speech from the Throne, the Government were praising themselves with the capital investments and projects here in Rarotonga and the Outer Islands.
Rakahanga was mentioned as one of the islands on these investment development projects. I was surprised, so I contacted the EO in case the project involving the construction of the Rakahanga Cyclone Centre was done in my absence. He replied nothing major has been done but much needed repairs to the school roofing and to the water tanks are required. The question is? Am I wrong or is there another project or infrastructure because I am asking this question on the grounds that the Rakahanga Council Members are holding meetings in the rundown administration office and I feel sorry for them. There are no toilet and kitchen facilities.
So please Minister, the only reason why I am asking you this question is because it was mentioned in the Speech from the Throne.
