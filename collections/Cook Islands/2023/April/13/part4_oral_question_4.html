Oral Question 4

MR T. HEATHER: Thank you Honourable Minister for your response. The reason why Mr Speaker is because the Minister confirmed yesterday that the Ministry of Corrective Services funded this machinery. The total amount expended for this machinery is $130,000. It is important for the Minister to know this instead of Votes numbers. From 2019 up until today, Mr Speaker, I cannot understand why a Minister will purchase a machine using taxpayers’ money and use it for his own purposes and not for the people of this country.
I was happy with the Prime Minister yesterday when he shared about the two children of his that works for the Ministry. He was correct when he said, “Father of the Nation, Father of the Government.” My question to you Honourable Prime Minister, these are your two children, and if they did something wrong under your Ministerial leadership, what would you do with regarding the funds that were misused by the Government? The machine stayed with him throughout General Election, which he used for his own purposes.
