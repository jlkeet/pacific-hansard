Oral Question 40

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is to the Minister for ICI.
I was very happy to read todays paper, the Minister outlined the work that will be done on our roads. I was also happy to note that Matavera has been included in these plans. Mr Speaker, my question is more a request.
The current plan will be carried out from Matavera and then to Titikaveka next month, back to Matavera the following month. My question to the Minister is in regards to the backroad of Matavera. This will include Tupapa, from the Manihiki Hostel all the way to Ngatangiia and then to the main road and then straight to Titikaveka; can the backroad be considered?
The article that was printed in the local newspaper was making referrals to a month-by-month work program especially the allocation of the works on our roads, including Matavera.
So, my question therefore is this. If possible Honourable Minister that the repairs of the back road of Matavera, if that could take place from the Manihiki hostel in Tupapa and all the way back to the other end of Matavera.
