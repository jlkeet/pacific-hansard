Oral Question 14

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you Mr Speaker. Thank you for the question from the Member for Murienua.
Just to shorten my reply Mr Speaker, the answer is no. I did not made any comments to that effect because when this project in Manihiki was tendered out to the public, there was only one company that responded to the tender and that is the TRIAD Company. To date, ICI and the TRIAD company is negotiating with the costing for the development of the airport because we all know in this short period of time there has been a change of prices for everything.
To recap my reply to the Member, I cannot recall making those comments but I can recall commenting the ICI working closely with the sole tender of the contract which is the TRIAD Company to finalise a final costing for the development of the airport in Manihiki. This week on Thursday, we will meet again with the TRIAD Company and hopefully we will have a final costing with TRIAD and an announcement will follow once that confirmation is made. Thank you.
