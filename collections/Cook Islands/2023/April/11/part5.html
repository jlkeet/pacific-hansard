OBITUARIES
Firstly, to our former Chaplain, Rev. Oirua Peter Rasmussen. Our deepest condolences to both families that are residing here in Rarotonga and overseas, may God comfort you all.
Secondly, to Papa Ben Beniamina Unu, from Atiu, who have passed away here in Rarotonga, on behalf of all Members of Parliament and the Honourable Minister and Honourable Member of Atiu, we extend our deepest condolences to the family and to everyone.
Thirdly, our sympathy to Papa Tutai Toru who have passed away on the island of Atiu; we would like to extend our gratitude and condolences to the families who are in bereavement on the island of Atiu. Some of us may know that Papa Tutai Toru used to live in Avatiu and he was the former Principal Immigration Officer here in Rarotonga back in those days.
