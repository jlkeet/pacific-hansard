COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
I have one announcement to make. I would like to remind Honourable Members that on Monday, 21st June 2021; I notified the House that the wearing of neckties on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays are optional. This ruling does not extend to the wearing of jackets, unless we decide otherwise. It also does not allow the wearing of jeans in the House. Thank you Honourable Members.
