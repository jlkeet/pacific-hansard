Oral Question 3

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question goes to the Minister of Finance, our Prime Minister. I believe my question is a simple question to you Honourable Minister of Finance.
My question is whether there is any allocation in our Budget for the Outer Islands to help with their 200 Years’ Bicentennial Celebration? First of all, the islands of Atiu, Mitiaro, Mauke and Rarotonga. Next year is Mangaia’s turn to celebrate their Bicentennial. But the reason why I wanted to ask this question is because I know that Aitutaki were allocated with some funds when they celebrated their Gospel anniversary back in 2021.
