Oral Question 5

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker and on this question, I am surprised because I hear that these islands have raised a lot of money in their fundraising for this anniversary event. It is not for them to ask government for funds, it is for government to ask them for money.
Mr Speaker, there is an allocation in the Ministry of Culture’s budget for big culture events like this annually. Major events like the Constitution or like the Cook Islands Games, that is what this portion of funds is put aside for.
The budget of the government in 2021 is for the celebration of the arrival of the Gospel to our country, Cook Islands. That was the national contribution to that national event of that year. Each islands celebration, then follows after the national celebration, that is each island own commemoration of the event.
The government’s financial contribution of that year, it was in the form of chairs, tents and artefacts to assist the Aitutaki, Gospel Anniversary. This year, we know the events is like a day after each one especially in Nga-Pu-Toru and immediately after that, on Rarotonga. So, it would be difficult to send those sorts same equipment’s to each of those islands, it would be very difficult.

But from what I have heard, the preparations are progressing well and each island has fundraised significant amount of money to be able to host their people when they come on to their island for each of those days’ events.
Also, the role of the government would be able to release government workers on each of these islands to be able to participate in order to be able to help festivities that will take place on each of these islands.
A special programme is also being made so that our schools and our school children on each of these islands will also be able to participate as a learning experience in their school year programme. So, it will be like a day off holiday as a learning experience.
It is through these little planning and programming that government’s hopes to assist our people and the celebration on each of the islands will be a memorable one.
