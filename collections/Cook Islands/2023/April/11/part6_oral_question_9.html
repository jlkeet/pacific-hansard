Oral Question 9

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you Mr Speaker for allowing me to respond to the question. Thank you Honourable Member of Matavera for your question. What am I going to do as Minister of the House of Ariki for the Gospel Bicentennial Celebration?
The first thing I am going to do on that day is, the Opening Ceremony for the Event and I will be welcoming the Chiefs and the Child of the Chiefs here in Rarotonga. I have received an invitation from Tinomana Ariki and her Office. I will also be taking a group of invitees to this major Event. As per the question raised on what I will be doing, the answer that I gave you is what I will be doing on that day.
