Oral Question 19

MRS A. ARMSTRONG: Thank you Mr Speaker. Greetings to everybody in the House today. Before I ask my question, I just wanted to briefly comment on the incentive for the reinstatement of the Community Policing program on Rarotonga. I just wanted to acknowledge the former Member of Parliament for Titikaveka, Selina Napa, for requesting the above incentive for reinstatement. I just wanted to say that this has been very productive, so thank you very much to the Government and especially to the Prime Minister.
My question is to the Prime Minister; sorry, you are in the firing line today. I just wanted to know how much is the Departure Tax on the airline tickets that we are collecting because the last time I knew was either $55 or $60. It is of interest to the public and I may have a Supplementary Question. Thank You.
