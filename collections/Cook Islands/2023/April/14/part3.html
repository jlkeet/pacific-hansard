Yes Mr Speaker, to answer her question, technically yes. However, I must go back to the Speech from the Throne.
Now, the portion that she mentioned yesterday was a part of a bigger part of the Speech. And I quote the Speech from the Crown, “we have funded a number of initiatives aimed at a growing economy reducing barriers and the competition growing labour force making government more efficient in the Pa Enua.”

When this is finished early May, which is in a next few weeks, the team will move to Rakahanga, specifically Mr Speaker, to redo the spouting on both the Hospital and the School. Also, the roofing is being procured for the Hospital and we would expect for this on the island in the next few months. The reroofing will be undertaken hopefully by July.
Also Mr Speaker, Rakahanga is one of the last remaining island requiring a Cyclone Centre, along with Nassau may I add. So, we have undertaken planning for the Rakahanga Cyclone Centre, the funding component for this particular project is still in progress. Also, that has been approved on the ICI front. There is a plan to repair for the Community water tanks for the Rakahanga CICC Hall, as well as the square tank next to the Church.
So, to answer the Honourable Member’s question, yes! I hope the Honourable Member would acknowledge and accept the information.
