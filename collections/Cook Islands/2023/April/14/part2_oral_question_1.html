Greetings to the Prime Minister, Deputy Prime Minister and all Cabinet Ministers and Members on the Government side. Greetings also to the Leader of the Opposition and Members of the Opposition and all other Members.
To the staff of Parliament, I do believe that you have worked very hard throughout this week so greetings to you all.
We will not forget our people living overseas in New Zealand, Australia, Tahiti and other parts of the world watching our livestream and listening to this broadcast, greetings to you all. Greetings also to our people here on the island of Rarotonga and the Outer Islands, greetings to you all.
We have no further notices, therefore, we will move on to Question Time and this is for 30 minutes. I now open the Floor for questions.
