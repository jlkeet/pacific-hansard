Report of the Business Committee relating to the Sitting Calendar for 2023.
I call the Honourable Deputy Chairman of the Business Committee, Tingika Elikana on the Floor.
MR T. ELIKANA: Thank you Mr Speaker. Thank you for letting me present the Report of the Business Committee. I know that you would love to present the Report but as agreed by all the Committee Members, I should present this.
As set out in the Report Mr Speaker, the Business Committee met on 29th March 2023 and that is to consider a Sitting programme and to recommend those dates to Parliament. I must emphasize Mr Speaker that those are just recommendations and based on the work and urgency of matters and the availability of Members, it might change.
I think all Members have copies of the Report and I am sure that all Members have read and are familiar with the recommended dates.
On that note Mr Speaker, I move:
That this Parliament take note of the Report of the Business Committee relating to the Sitting Calendar for 2023
