Oral Question 21

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker. I think this question, from memory and I am not quite accurate, I would like to come back with an accurate figure but I think it is about $62 at this stage. In the Departure Tax Act, there is a provision each year for an incremental increase in departure tax. So, rather than after a period, we increase the departure tax which can cause difficulties with the airlines who collect this in their ticketing. When we passed our Departure Tax Act, we put that provision in so that each year, there is a slight increase and it provides assurance in planning for the airlines to know what the departure tax amount is that they need to factor into their tickets.
