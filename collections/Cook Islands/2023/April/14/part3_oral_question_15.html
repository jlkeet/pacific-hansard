Oral Question 15

MR R. HEATHER: So, my question is for you Honourable Prime Minister. A few days ago, I heard the Member from Titikaveka talk about Community Policing because back in 2008, the Community Policing program that was established in the Puaikura district, were stationed in the Community Hall. They had been using the Community Hall as their office for eight years without rental payments. However, in 2016, this program came to a halt. The question to you Mr Prime Minister is, whether Government is thinking of reviving the Community Policing program again.
