Oral Question 17

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you very much for the very good question, raised by our Member for Akaoa.
In fact, our Caucus had a discussion just a few weeks ago regarding improving the presence of our Police in our communities, particularly here on Rarotonga. I can advise, and in my discussion with the new Police Commissioner, he is also very supportive of the establishment of our Community Policing program. So, we will be working very closely with our community group to re-establish our Community Police within our districts as soon as possible. This is also a request that is coming through from our communities as well. That is, they want a presence of Police in the community to help deter any petty crimes in particular but also to project a presence of safety within the community. So, I look forward to the establishment of the Community Police increasing a presence hopefully along the policy of not paying any rent to the Community Hall. Thank you Mr Speaker.
