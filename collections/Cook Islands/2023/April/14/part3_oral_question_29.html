Oral Question 29

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker. In regards to the issue of marijuana weed, you are referring to; well I believe the Police have just completed a major drug bust in Aitutaki, which resulted in a number of convictions of people on the island of Aitutaki. I do know the Police have their active team working on this issue as well. Nevertheless, these are matters that are sensitive to the Police and not matters that they will share or divulge with their Minister. From what I believe and understand Mr Speaker is that their work is progressing as it has been in the past with their detection services and the drug dogs and so forth. That is about the extent I know. Thank you.
