OBITUARIES
Mama Orometua Hakono Saitu, who had passed away on the island of Manihiki on 18 April 2023, in their Tauhunu CICC Ekalesia. Mama Orometua Hakono Saitu is the daughter of Papa Tokotini Nanua and Mama Kura Tokotini Nanua. She is from the Ariki family Faingahitu of the island of Manihiki. We would like to extend our condolences to Papa Orometua Tumukahu Saitu, their children and all the families. These words of condolences is from the Honourable Member for Manihiki, Akaiti Puna and all Honourable Members of this House.
