Oral Question 8

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. I have a Supplementary Question. Honourable Minister, you said that the Ministry of Education is working on the new Master Plan for the next 10 years. Mr Speaker, I have gone and study the allocation for the Ministry of Education and the distribution of their allocation.
I would like to propose a recommendation to the Minister and the Secretary of Education, if dialects and languages of each islands can be included in this Master Plan and to be taught in our schools. The schools in the Northern Group islands to teach their island languages, the islands in the Southern Group to each their dialects and even on Rarotonga, to teach their dialect.
For example, the Pukapuka language to be taught from Grade 1 to Form 2 on the island. I was told by one of the Members that the language spoken in Mangaia is a total mixture. So, I am asking the Minister and the Secretary of Education to include our islands dialects in your new Master Plan. The Northern Group languages, the Southern Group and the Rarotonga dialects to be taught in our schools.
The question to the Minister is, whether this request can be included in your new Education Master Plan for the next 10 years?
