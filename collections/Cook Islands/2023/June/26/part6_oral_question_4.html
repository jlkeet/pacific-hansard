Oral Question 4

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. Thank you Honourable Prime Minister for your answer to my question. I believe that this is an important question because we were just discussing about the Vote Item for this Ministry.
My question now Mr Speaker is to the Minister of Education. According to the Report sent to the Public Service Commission, it is a good Report because it is classified as ‘unmodified’. According to this Report, their Strategic Master plan for the schools will come to an end in 2023.
The question to the Minister is, what is your Master Plan that you have for the future of our education?
