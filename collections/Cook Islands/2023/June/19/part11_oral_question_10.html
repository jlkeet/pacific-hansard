Oral Question 10

MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Tina Pupue Browne. I agree with you and first opportunity tomorrow, I will give the Floor to you and not the Honourable Vaitoti Tupa.
Honourable Members, as this is Question Time, I think the problem here is you Honourable Members; your questions and answers are like stories. It is up to you; you either ask your question, either a closed-ended question or open-ended question, so that we can get a simpler answer.
But then again, we Polynesians are not like Europeans who asks close-ended questions. We like to tell long stories and when the answer is given, it comes with more stories. We are different from the European ways and thinking. Nevertheless, I will leave it to you to decide. I feel sorry for the Leader of the Opposition because she did not have time to ask her question. Therefore, Honourable Members, this is an encouragement to you all to be wise with our Question Time.
So, do not Point of Order any Member who have the Floor because it will complicate our procedures. Thank you Honourable Members.
