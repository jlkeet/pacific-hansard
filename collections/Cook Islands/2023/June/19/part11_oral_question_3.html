Oral Question 3

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker and thank you to the Member for Matavera for the question. We recall during COVID when our boarders closed, the Government looked for ways to respond and to try and create an economic activity in the absence of any tourism industry. One of the initiatives was to encourage people to use technology to try and find ways to be innovative and create business. One of the ways Mr Speaker was to develop agriculture industries, but using new modern technology. These were designed as support in terms of grant funding for individuals or entrepreneurs to be able to come up with some projects and I do believe that there was a number of our people who put forward applications to the Committee that was established to try and receive some of this grant funding to get their projects up and running.
Now those were one-off fundings that we have in terms of response. We know that once our borders opened we shifted our focus to economic recovery. So, the question is if I am right that are there any funding’s going towards the AgriTech Sector in this budget. The reports on the AgriTech funding Mr Speaker are available to any Member in the House on the Ministry of Finance MFEM website and the Members will be able to have a look at who received these funding and what sort of projects were developed.
However, in terms of what funding is available in this Budget towards AgriTech Mr Speaker, the budget is before the House in debate and the Bill before the House in debate should not have questions asked for it in Question Time. Thank you Mr Speaker.
