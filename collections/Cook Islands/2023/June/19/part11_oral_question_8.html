Oral Question 8

HON. T. HEATHER: My question is directed to the Minister of ICI and it goes with an explanation so he will understand the reason of my question, and not to the Minister of Corrective Services. Continuing with my line of questioning is whether it is possible to delay the road tarsealing until after the opening of the Church? I could see the Minister travelling up and down the road yesterday. Maybe he did not realise it is a Sunday and is a day of worship for us.
There are work machineries parked in the Church compound at Arorangi Mr Speaker. The request Honourable Minister for ICI is, if it is possible to cease the sealing today and remove the machineries so we can carry on with setting up the Church for the Opening? I am asking on behalf of the Congregation to cease the work and to pick up again on 25th June 2023. This would then give us ample time to set up for the opening of the Church and then come on Thursday you will see what I am talking about. That is my request Honourable Minister.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member. I see the Honourable Minister; you have the Floor.
