Oral Question 9

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Before I respond to the question from the Honourable Member from Murienua, if I may Mr Speaker say a brief comment to you this afternoon. If I may say, Kia Orana Mr Speaker and your staff this afternoon. A Kia Orana to all of us in the House, to us Members of Parliament who have been elected to serve our people. Also, Kia Orana to all our people listening in to us this afternoon.
Mr Speaker, in respond to the question from the Honourable Member, may I give a short explanation so we can all be in the same picture of how this situation arose. Mr Speaker, a few weeks ago, I was paid a visit by Mama Tinomona Ariki, accompanied by her orator Tere Taio and one of the Deacons of the Church, Smiley Heather. This is the request by Mama Tinomona to me, “My boy, is it possible with your approval if the road can be sealed in time for the upcoming 200- year commemoration?” I did not hesitate to reply Mr Speaker, straight away, I said “yes.”
I then asked Mama Tinomona that morning to give me at least a day in order for me to look into how this request will become a reality.
Mr Speaker, the reason why I did not hesitated in responding is because when I looked at Mama Tinomona, I saw her as a Mama for all of Puaikura. In my belief, her request was so influential. So, this is what happened. As we know, I managed to speak to Chris Vaile of Triad, asking for assistance in request from Mama Tinomana.
However, during this discussion, I found out that one of Mama Tinomona’s Mataiapo had already approached Chris Vaile for assistance on sealing the road. This Mataiapo is one of the landowners of the quarry where the aggregates are being mined by Triad. Chris Vaile assured me not to worry as there is an agreement between him and the Mataiapo to seal the road. He also assured me that it will not cost the taxpayers a cent on the Mamas request for the road sealing.
This is the plan Mr Speaker on this special request at this short notice; we have to act immediately because there is not enough time for us to do this upgrade for this period. This is the plan; ICI will facilitate the program and we will manage the project using our ICI engineers to get this project done as requested by Tinomana Ariki. TRIAD and Papa Tauei Solomona will take care of the rest of the project.
Therefore, this is our current situation hence we had started immediately because there is not enough time to do this work in preparation for the 200 Years Celebration. I would like to apologise, not to the Honourable Member but to the village of Puaikura. For any inconvenience caused, please bear with me and I apologised for this was a request from Mama Tinomana Ariki.
