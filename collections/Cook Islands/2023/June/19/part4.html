COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
I have some announcements to make.
Firstly, I would like to offer to you all, the Clerks’ apology for his absence in the House this afternoon. The Clerk has been summoned to Court as a witness; therefore, he is unable to attend Parliament at this time. The Clerk will join us when the Court no longer requires his service. In the interim, the Deputy Clerk will assume all responsibilities performed by the Clerk. Thank you for your understanding.
Secondly, I would like to convey our congratulations to Mr Papaterai William for being appointed as the new Editor of Debates for Parliament; may our Heavenly Father continue to bless you and provide you the knowledge and wisdom as you serve in your new role. As you may have noticed, the Sub-Editor of Debates’ position has been advertised.
Finally, we also o our congratulations to our Parliament IT Consultant, Mr Joe Wichman for being awarded a Certificate of Excellence in UAV Piloting Certification from Aviation Training Institute. Joe received the top award first equal with a student from China; Joe was the only Pacific Islander out of 167 students in the course. Congratulations Joe, may our Heavenly Father continue to grant you the wisdom and knowledge as you serve our people in your professional career.
