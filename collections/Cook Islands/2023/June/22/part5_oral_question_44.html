Oral Question 44

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you Mr Speaker. To answer the question from the Member for Matavera, the expert of the prison is telling me he was only 14 when he was put into prison and today, he is the Honourable Minister of that Prison. Under our law, when you are 16 years, the lawyer can ask the offender to stand in the higher court. Under our law also, when you are 16 years, your case can be heard in the delinquent court. If you are 18 years old, then your case will be taken to the high court.
Mr Speaker, I know my time is up but if I can explain the case further. Under the administration of the Ministry of Justice today, they are working towards the revival of the Delinquent Court. I want to assure this House the appropriation of the Ministry of Justice in this Budget will assure the revival of this Childrens Court in the near future.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Minister.
