Oral Question 30

MRS T. MATAPO: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is going to the Minister of Health. I believe this is not something new but I was asked to follow this up so that is what I am doing. During the time of COVID, our disabled children classroom was asked to give this chamber to the Ministry of Health.
The disabled children took their leave and moved to another place for their meetings. The request that I convey from our disable management in Mangaia; if the Ministry of Health or the government can reimburse the amount of money, they spent in renovating the place which the Health is using; an amount of $8,000. This money was raised through a telethon.
The question to you Minister is if the money can be paid to our disabled children because these children are meeting for their special classes on the veranda of one of the homes in the village. There is a vacant house that they can use but it needs to be renovated to be a healthy place for our disabled children to have their special classes. If the funds can be made available, we will be able to properly fix this house to be a permanent meeting place or classroom for our special needs children on Mangaia.
That is the question to you Minister; thank you.
