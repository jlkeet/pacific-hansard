Oral Question 36

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you Mr Speaker. Thank you very much to the Honourable Member for Matavera for your question. I understand that two of your inmate friends told you that there is not enough sleeping areas in prison. Since I became the Minister Responsible for the Prison in 2018, there were 68 inmates. In those days, they were sleeping in beds. Today, there are 27 prisoners in our prison. I cannot understand your question Honourable Member as to whether your claim is from prisoners in prison or just friends out of prison.
To answer your question, there is enough sleeping rooms in our prison today. The prison is good and it does not need any more appropriation for its management. Thank you.
