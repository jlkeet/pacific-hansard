Oral Question 17

MR. T. ISAMAELA: Kia Orana Mr Speaker and extending to the Government and the Opposition. I want to applaud the Member Vaitoti Tupa because he is not here or else he will take most of the talking time.
My question is directed to the Leader of the country, the Prime Minister.
Prime Minister, I want you to explain to the House concerning our people collecting the New Zealand pension on whether they are being taxed or not? I am asking in relation to all Cook Islanders because in the last Parliament Sitting, we passed a law on the tax-free threshold for the Outer Islands. Is it then only the pensioners from the Outer Islands that will not be taxed?
