COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
I have an announcement to make and this notice is to inform Honourable Members that we will be adjourning Parliament today earlier than our normal adjournment time at 5.00 p.m. and this is to enable those of you who have been invited to attend the opening of the newly renovated Church for CICC in Arorangi. At 3.50 p.m., I will cease the business of the House and ask the Leader of the House to move the adjournment Motion. Thank you Honourable Members.
I call the Clerk to read the list of Papers for today.
