Oral Question 27

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: A question for the Prime Minister. We talked about recession the other day in New Zealand. I understand that one of the factors or one of the aspects that has shrunk in New Zealand is the exports. I took that to mean, Prime Minister that the level of export from New Zealand to the receivers have dropped.
Is there any thinking of negotiating with New Zealand to get better deals for us if they are not able to export to their normal recipients? Would they be considering off-loading to us at cheaper prices?
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Prime Minister; you have the Floor.
