Oral Question 2

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is directed to the Leader of the Opposition. Yesterday, the Leader of the Opposition posed a question to the Minister of Agriculture where she mis-represented what the Minister of Agriculture had said in reference to Ministerial travel.
The Leader of the Opposition suggested that the Minister of Agriculture had stated that Ministerial travels are “fully funded”. Upon perusal of the Hansard record, Mr Speaker, it quite clearly states in the Hansard of Page 16 that the Minister stated, “some of these travels are funded.” So my question to the Leader of the Opposition is, is she going to apologize to the Minister of Agriculture and to this House for mis-representing the statement made by the Minister of Agriculture?
