Oral Question 11

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is to the Prime Minister. Can you please explain on the formation of Boards and the Boards under CIIC? I am asking this question to him because he was the previous Minister for CIIC before the Minister Albert Nicholas. Prime Minister, we know that the Board for Power Supply on Araura is under the control of CIIC.
We also heard from the Member of Amure/Ureia last week on the finance controlled by the Power Supply Board is under the CIIC. So, that is the question, if you can give some clarification on it and I believe some Board chairs on Rarotonga are listening in so they know the reasons of the Board coming together. Thank you.
