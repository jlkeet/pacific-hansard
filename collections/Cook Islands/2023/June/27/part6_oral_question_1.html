Oral Question 1

MR R. HEATHER: Thank you Mr Speaker. Before I pose my question, I seek leave for the Honourable Teariki Heather, Member for Murienua, who is currently overseas for yesterday and today.
As I did not have time to speak yesterday on the Environment Vote Item and this morning, I direct my question to the Minister for NES, National Environment Services. This question is in regards to our foreshore issues from the beaches of Muri and right to Puaikura. There is an allocation of $400,000 funding for researching into this environmental issue in our nation.
The question to you Minister is, is there an allocation of funds by the Government to help our landowners in these beach areas from business owners that are running their businesses on the beaches? Thank you.
