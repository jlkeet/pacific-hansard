Oral Question 7

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Speaker. I will go back to our Bible text that was referred to us this morning. It says, “in the eyes of God we are all the same, Jews and Gentiles.” When I look at this Appropriation, it is the same for Government and for the Gentiles, all of us will benefit under this Appropriation. Under our law, all is equal.
This new law change will only affect the Outer Islands and will not apply to Rarotonga. We know what life is like on the Pa Enua and when you purchase goods in the shops, the cost is so much higher there than here on Rarotonga. A carton of chicken in the Pa Enua costs more than in Rarotonga. The carton of green bottles costs higher in the Pa Enua. The ice cream, bread and other things. This is the purpose of this law change to alleviate the burden that is placed on our people in the Pa Enua.
So, it is not like the Outer Islands are ahead of us here in Rarotonga, no. We are leveling the playing field as much as we can to ease the burden on those who live in the Pa Enua. I hope that clarifies the position Mr Speaker and Honourable Member; thank you.
