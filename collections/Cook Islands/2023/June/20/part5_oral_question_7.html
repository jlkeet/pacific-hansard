Oral Question 7

HON. V. R. TOKI-BROWN: Kia Orana to all Honourable Members in this House. Kia Orana to you Mr Speaker, your staff, and the Leaders of our Parties.
Let me answer your question Honourable Member by explaining our present situation. Our problem at this time is that our barge is not here in the Cook Islands, it is currently sitting overseas for service. Therefore, we do not have a barge to ship the excavator to Mangaia.
The Minister of ICI wants to use this excavator here on Rarotonga but I am holding on to it for Mangaia as promised to help our growers there. Thank you.
