Oral Question 5

MRS T. MATAPO: Thank you Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to all the Honourable Members of this House. Before I ask my question, I have a humble request to make Mr Speaker, a moment for me to convey my condolences to a family from my village for a sudden death of a family member, which have occurred here in Rarotonga.
To the family of Mama Metu Aurupa for the passing away of your daughter Mama Moeroa Pinaki, my deepest condolences to you all. This is my next-door neighbour in the village of Tamarua. Therefore, to the Papa Orometua, the husband and all the families, may the Lord blesses you all during this time.

Yesterday, there were two very popular Members on that side of the House, the Minister for Finance and the Minister for ICI. Today, sitting between them is a beautiful rose, which balances the atmosphere in their setting.
My question is going out to the Minister of Health and Agriculture. At this time, the island of Mangaia is preparing to plant their crops to be ready for the Bicentennial next year. This is my direct question as encouraged by the Speaker yesterday to the Honourable Minister. The excavator, which you have promised for Mangaia, when will that arrive on the island of Mangaia? I want the people of my island to hear the answer directly from you.
