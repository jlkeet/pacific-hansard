Oral Question 24

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker. Greetings to everyone. I have a short question to the Minister of Seabed Mineral. This morning, the Prime Minister and I together with Minister Mokoroa went to the presentation by the Seabed Mineral at Crown Beach Hotel. I felt sorry for the presentation by the Seabed because I believe they have taken a long time to prepare their presentation. But because not many people attended, the presentation started late which means that they had to speed up their presentation.
I found the presentation very educational and helpful because the Licensees explained what work is being done on their research boats.
My question Prime Minister, I am not sure if the people on the other side was invited to this presentation because I believe that it is at this venue, if you have a question then you can ask your question. We know there are some people who speak against this project and to me, I believe this is the time to ask questions.
