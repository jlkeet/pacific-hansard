Oral Question 8

HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you Mr Speaker. I believe that once this House approves this Vote Item, then we will begin with this feasibility study. To give an answer as to when this work will start is not too certain now but I do believe until we get the results of the feasibility report and recommendations, then we will be looking for fundings for these projects. It will be then, we will be able to confirm when the work will commence. But I would like to assure us in the House today Mr Speaker, I will be on this issue weekly and just like our road program, I will be consistently pushing, pushing and pushing. Thank you.
