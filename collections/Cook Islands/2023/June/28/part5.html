Mr Speaker, to the question on the $400,000 budget proposed allocation to the National Environment Service regarding the coastal management feasibility assessment for Rarotonga and Aitutaki, may I Mr Speaker, express the need to identify the risks and concerns relating to exposure to coastal hazards and how well our current coastal protection is working. From there Mr Speaker, we will develop guidelines that will help NES evaluate costal management proposals for future environment impact assessments or EIA.
