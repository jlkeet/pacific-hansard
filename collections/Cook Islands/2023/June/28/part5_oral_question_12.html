Oral Question 12

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is directed to the Minister for ICI. I am sure we have talked about this topic some time ago. So, the question is if the Minister for ICI can explain to us on the standard of the dump on Rarotonga and if there are any plans of purchasing an incinerator. We had talked about this beforehand and I am wondering if there are any updates from the Minister for ICI? If the Minister gives an affirmative reply, then the Supplementary Question is then how long will it take for the incinerator to arrive? Thank you.
