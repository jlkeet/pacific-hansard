Oral Question 5

MR T. ISAMAELA: Yes, I want to clarify my question first. The Prime Minister stated that the Opposition was not in support of this tax system for the Outer Islands. I want this House to know that I am not on the Opposition; I am in the middle. I was one of the first Member to support this tax reform because I represent the Outer Islands. My question to the Honourable Prime Minister, can he explain again to this House whether a discussion with New Zealand regarding New Zealand Pensioners here in the Cook Islands took place? I wonder whether our people will face any issues in the future regarding this.
I remember back in 2013, it is an income for those who collecting the pension.
Thank you Mr Speaker.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member. I see that you have the same shirt as your two colleagues from Aitutaki.
