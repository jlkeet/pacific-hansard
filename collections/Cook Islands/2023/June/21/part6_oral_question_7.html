Oral Question 7

HON. V. MOKOROA: Thank you to the Member for Tamarua for this question. In short, the current budget that we have does not entertain the thought about re-introducing our Teacher’s Training College. However, staff within the Ministry are currently consulting the next 10-year plan for the Ministry of Education. If the appetite and the emphasis within the next 10-year plan is encouraging our Government of the day to re-introduce our Teachers Training College, then I can anticipate that happening in the next master plan for the Ministry of Education.
Therefore, my understanding is the Ministry of Education is travelling around the Outer Islands to undertake the consultation for this 10-year plan and if our Members in the Outer Islands or anybody listening to this discussion to please see our Ministry of Education and encourage them to re- introduce the Teacher’s Training College. I hope this answers the question. Thank you.
