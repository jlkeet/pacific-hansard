COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE SPEAKER
I would like to announce in the House this afternoon about the presentation by the Seabed Mineral Authority to the Members of Parliament next Monday, 26 June at 12.00 p.m. in our dining room. The presentation will provide an update of significant developments over the last year. Members may recall the Honourable Prime Minister mentioned about this presentation in the House yesterday. Light lunch will be provided for Members to have while the presentation is progressing.
The arrangement for us in terms of our Sitting for next Monday is that, when we adjourn on Friday 23 June, a Motion will be put to the House for the House to adjourn until 1.30 p.m. Monday 26 June. This will allow the Seabed Mineral Team one hour for their presentation.
Secondly, please if you Honourable Members could make time during our Session or lunch break to have your individual photos taken for your official profiles, so comb your hair properly. Please see Unuia and Marina for these to be taken. Thank you again Honourable Members.
