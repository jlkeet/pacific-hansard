Oral Question 9

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker and all Members of this House, greetings to you all. To those people listening to the radio and watching the livestream, Kia Orana. My question is directed to the Minister Responsible for Parliamentary Services.

Yesterday there was a mention in the House on Ministerial travels overseas on Government businesses. The comments were that these travels are fully funded. Therefore, my interpretation is that there are no expenditures from the Government coffer to cover any travel expenses. I am requesting the Minister to give an in-depth explanation on this because when we look at the budget, funds have been put aside for Ministerial travels. However, if I may assist in that, I am aware that all Ministers and Members travelling overseas have to account for and declare any expenses they make to Parliament.
So, I stand to be corrected Minister on what I heard you said in the House yesterday.
Thank you.
