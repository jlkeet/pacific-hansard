Oral Question 11

HON. V. R. TOKI-BROWN: Thank you. Kia Orana Mr Speaker, Clerk and all your staff including all our people and us Members.
In response to the question from the Leader of the Opposition, I can recall mentioning yesterday that some of the overseas travels are paid. I also believe this is not confidential because they are included in the allocations under the Civil List in the House. This also goes to the travels of Heads of Ministries. However, some of the travels are funded from abroad. Some also come from within their allocated budgets.
