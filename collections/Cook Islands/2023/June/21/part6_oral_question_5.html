Oral Question 5

MRS T. MATAPO: Thank you Honourable Speaker and greetings to all Members of this House. I have a Supplementary Question for the Minister of Education but before that, thank you for your explanations for what is being done for our education system. My question Minister is, if there are any plans to re-start the Teachers Training College. At the moment, the training and the studies are coming out of USP and I believe that this is a good thing. But the idea that I want to raise is that the students should start off with the thought that they want to become teachers. That is the question that I want to ask.
