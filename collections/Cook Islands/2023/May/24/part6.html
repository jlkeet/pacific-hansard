COMMUNICATION FROM THE SPEAKER
I have a few announcements to make.
Firstly, on behalf of our Members of Parliament, Clerk and Staff of Parliament, we would like to extend our words of encouragement and well wishes to our hard-working Transcriber, Mrs Tai Hosking, who is currently in New Zealand for health treatment. We send you our positive thoughts and love and we look forward to having you back with us very soon.
I would also like to welcome two new Staff Members to our Parliamentary family. Our new Senior Finance Officer, Mrs Grace Ioane and Assistant Administration and Finance Officer, Ms Marina Harry; we greet and welcome you both to our working family and may your journey with Parliament be an enjoyable and successful one.
The last couple of days have been extremely busy for our Public Accounts Committee. Later in our Sitting today, the Chairman for PAC will be tabling four Committee Reports to the House.
I would like to take this opportunity to thank the Director of Audit, Mr Desmond Wilden and also the Advisor to the Public Accounts Committee for his contribution and unwavering support to the Committee.
Last but not least, due to the passing of our former Chaplain Reverend Oirua Rasmussen, I am delighted to formerly announce the new Chaplain for Parliament, Reverend Teava Nanai. Reverend Teava is also the new Minister for the Nikao CICC Church. We welcome Reverend Nanai and we thank you for your continuous service to our Parliament. May God bless you and your wife as you serve our people.
Now, we come to the Presentation of Reports of Parliamentary Committees.
