Question time is for half an hour and I see the Honourable Member Vaitoti Tupa; you have the Floor.
MR. V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is to the Minister of ICI. I have an easy question for the Minister. Honourable Minister, the road from BCI in town to Avana is very smooth. My questions is, do you have any plans in applying hot-mixed for the road from the bridge of Avana to Titikaveka? We all know that Ngatangiia is one of the busiest village in Rarotonga. I would like to help the Honourable Member of Ngatangiia with his plea for the complete repair of the main road of his village, hence my question, if the Minister has any plans to complete the roadwork project to Titikaveka with hot-mixed. Thank you.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member. I see the Honourable Minister; you have the Floor.
HON. A. NICHOLAS: Thank you for your question. Before I respond to the question, Mr Speaker, Clerk and your staff members, Honourable Members of Parliament, Kia Orana to you all. In response to the question raised by the Honourable Member of Matavera, here is the simple answer to your simple question, Yes!
As per the plans in completing the roadwork project that was set last year, from Matavera to Avana, however, at that time before this major project started, a change of plan was made to cover the main road from Matavera to Tupapa first. However, we can see today that this work is completed and the work will pick up again from Avana and will continue to Titikaveka.
I would like to inform our people that the intention of the Government is to complete the entire roadwork projects for Rarotonga. It is true that some villages have been left out, but to let everyone know, the intention of the Government is to complete the whole of Rarotonga with hot-mixed, including Puaikura.
However, this depends on the funding we get; whenever we receive some funds, roadwork will continue.
Therefore, to answer your question, yes! Today, we have engineers from ICI preparing plans for this specific task for Avana to Muri and all the way to Titikaveka.
Once we get more funding, the project will continue on for the rest of Titikaveka and into Puaikura. And then from Puaikura, we will connect through to Harley Street, and that will complete our vision that we are aiming for.

MR T. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. I have a Supplementary Question and is directed to the Minister Responsible for ICI.
During the last Parliament Sitting, I was happy to know that the road sealing project will commence again and not only for Matavera, but all the way to Titikaveka and on the back road as well. The question then Honourable Minister is in reference to all the work done on the road. We know that the main contractor responsible for the hot-mixed sealing is Chris Vaile.
The question is, have you any thoughts in looking at involving other contractors such as the T&M Heather or Koko Ariihee’s company because they maintain our roads as well?
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member. I see the Honourable Minister; you have the Floor.
HON. A. NIHOLAS: Thank you Honourable Member for the good question. I can see the Minister Maggie is not here so the Member is hurling the questions at me instead.
Just to answer the question Honourable Member, yes there is a tender process within our system and within our works program. This is a process run by MFEM and the Crown Law Office. I believe Mr Speaker, when these main road programs were put up for tender, I believe TRIAD was the only company that submitted tenders for this particular hot-mixed sealing program.
But for our back roads Mr Speaker and our side roads, that is left totally up to our Civil team at ICI. The roads on our Pa Enua are also under the care of the Civil team at ICI and it is this program Mr Speaker that we draw the support and resources from our private sector such as the T&M Heather who are currently been engaged to deliver some of these roading programs.
Other companies such as Mike Rennie Contractors and Koko Ariihee has often been called on this program Mr Speaker. We have got four or five roads being repaired currently and these companies I have mentioned Mr Speaker, are all part of the work program that is currently running as we speak.
