Oral Question 13

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Kia Orana Mr Speaker and all the staff of Parliament. Kia Orana to the Prime Minister and your Cabinet Ministers, Honourable Members of the CIP and Independent Members. Kia Orana also to our side of the House, the United Party and the OCI Party and us the Democratic Party Members.
I have a question to the Prime Minister. Last week, a meeting was held in Rakahanga and the meeting was livestreamed. The purpose of the meeting was to inform the people that $37.5 million will be used to complete some of the outstanding projects that the Government did not complete. This includes the airport, solar system, water supply and everything else which will cost $37.5 million. The meeting was okay, however, the announcement that was made was that the Prime Minister supported this. The people that spoke in this meeting Prime Minister were the Mayor, EO and Iro Puna.
I am sorry Honourable Prime Minister because your name has been mentioned in the meeting so, I would like to ask you these questions:
1.Did you know about this meeting? 2.Did you know what the meeting was about? 3.Did you support the purpose of this meeting?
