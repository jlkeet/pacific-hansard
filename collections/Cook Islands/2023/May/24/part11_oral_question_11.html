Oral Question 11

I am unsure of where the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) stands between the Island Council and CIIC for this programme, but I wanted to assure you that one of the intentions in electing a new Chairman is to ensure that these groups of peoples to work together.

However, as I have told the Mayor and the EO, they have certain responsibilities that they need to do to bring everyone together and to ensure that this programme is completed. The intention of bringing the three Members of Parliament of Aitutaki together involves the Island Council as well.
