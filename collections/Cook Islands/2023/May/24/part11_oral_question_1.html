Oral Question 1

MR. V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is to the Minister of ICI. I have an easy question for the Minister. Honourable Minister, the road from BCI in town to Avana is very smooth. My questions is, do you have any plans in applying hot-mixed for the road from the bridge of Avana to Titikaveka? We all know that Ngatangiia is one of the busiest village in Rarotonga. I would like to help the Honourable Member of Ngatangiia with his plea for the complete repair of the main road of his village, hence my question, if the Minister has any plans to complete the roadwork project to Titikaveka with hot-mixed. Thank you.
