Oral Question 9

MR T. ISAMAELA: Thank you Mr Speaker. Greetings to all the Honourable Members of this House. To all those in Government and also those in the Opposition side, I greet you.
My question is also directed to the Minister Responsible for CIIC. I wanted to talk on our road in Aitutaki but because a “NO” was given as an answer to the comments by the Honourable Vaitoti Tupa, I then changed my mind.
Mr Speaker, I will make a brief explanation before asking my question. I am talking about the harbour project underway on Aitutaki. I am not criticizing the Government but I want to talk about the problems on the works done on the island.
We are still having problems on the island and the working spirit is unbecoming of how the work is managed. We also know that a lot of funding has been poured into this project. Ongoing discussions between the project managers and Island Government are rarely being held but if problems arise, then they go back to the discussion table. There are also problems amongst the workers working on the project. The only good thing that has happened is that appointment of the new Chairman of the CIIC Board and the question to the Minister is, what are your plans to settle these problems between the Island Government and the people of Aitutaki? How much money is required to complete this project? We know there is a new Chairman, I am asking how best can we run this project on Aitutaki?
