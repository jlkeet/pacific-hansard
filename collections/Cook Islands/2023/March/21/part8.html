ELECTION OF THE DEPUTY SPEAKER
Honourable Members, we will continue with the business of the House. According to Standing Orders 06 (g), we will go on to the election of the Deputy Speaker.
I see the Honourable Prime Minister, Honourable Mark Brown; you have the Floor.
HON. M. BROWN: Kia Orana to you Mr Speaker and may I offer you my congratulations on your appointment as the Speaker of the House. Of course, a good Speaker always has a very good Deputy.
In that spirit Mr Speaker, I move:
That the Honourable Tingika Elikana, Member of Parliament for Pukapuka/Nassau, do take the position of the Deputy Speaker of this Parliament
MR SPEAKER: I see the Honourable Member Vaitoti Tupa; you have the Floor.
MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker for giving me the opportunity to support the name that is being nominated for the Deputy Speaker.
Before I continue with my speech, I would like to congratulate you and thank you for accepting this position and for continuing to serve in this Honourable House as the Speaker of our Parliament and to each Members that have taken their Oath to serve this House.
