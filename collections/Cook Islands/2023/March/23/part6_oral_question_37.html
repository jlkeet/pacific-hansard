Oral Question 37

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker. My nephew answered a question which was never asked. I am grateful however, to his explanation as to the way forward.
And could I be afforded the same privilege to explain what I understand the position to be with regard to the enduring agreement that was reached by the parties in court three weeks ago?
MR SPEAKER: Honourable Member, I do not think that I will agree with the issue being debated.
HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Mr Speaker, it is not a debate, it is just stating the facts.
