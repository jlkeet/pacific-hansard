Oral Question 12

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: Thank you Mr Speaker. Before I ask my question, hearing you announce the obituaries this afternoon, I want to give condolences from the Democratic Party to our family at the Manihiki Hostel for Nga Vakatini Greig. The family is waiting to travel to Manihiki on the barge, but we are unsure when the barge will leave, but it is scheduled to depart today. His wife and family are at the Manihiki Hostel and we want to pass on our condolences to the family. Even though this brother lives on Manihiki, he is also from Rakahanga.

The Supplementary Question from the Leader of the Cook Islands United is regarding the money that was refunded.
I heard the answer from the Prime Minister, he did not think that any money has been lost but he believes the money that was lost is the cash paid to the lawyer. Here is the question, how much money was lost in paying the lawyer?
