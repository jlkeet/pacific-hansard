OPENING PRAYER
MR SPEAKER (T. TURA): Honourable Members; please be seated. I call the House to order.
Kia Orana to all of us this afternoon. It is common practice for this House to greet the Members, so I take the honour to greet the Honourable Prime Minister and Honourable Ministers on the Government side. I further extend a very warm welcome to the Leader of the Opposition, Honourable Tina Browne and your supporters. Included in this greeting is the United Party Members and our Independent Member for Amuri/Ureia, Toanui Isamaela and all those who are present in the House this afternoon; greetings to you all.
I can see a few members of the public in the Public Gallery, a very warm welcome to you all. To the Clerk of Parliament and all your staff who are here to support this Sitting, greetings to you all.
A special and warm welcome to our people listening in and watching our livestream. People of Rarotonga, the different pillars that we always greet, the Leaders of our Churches and all the rest of our people, a very warm welcome to you all.
Greeting also go to the people of the Northern and Southern Group Islands and those from outside of the Cook Islands, greetings to you all in the Name of our Lord.
