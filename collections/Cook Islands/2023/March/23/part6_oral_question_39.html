Oral Question 39

HON. T. BROWNE: Very short, Mr Speaker.
The negotiation started in December, last year. And all the parties were keen to make it happen by the end of March. The reason for that is because of the escalating cost of materials. However, during the months that followed, we have to go through three plans. The first plan that was provided to the landowners was of an area of approximately 24 hectares. The second plan changed, it was amended to 20 hectares. Then, the third and final plan was 18 hectares. That is the reason for the delay, not because the landowners were dragging their feet, it was because the final plan had not been finalised.
