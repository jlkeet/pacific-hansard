Oral Question 41

MRS A. ARMSTRONG: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is not as exciting as what was previously asked. Before I ask my question, I would like to comment on the attire or the tie of our Honourable Member George Angene. I love those yellow shells as they are all the way from Mangaia.
My question is to the Honourable Prime Minister or the Minister Responsible for Parliament. My question is in this Sitting of the 18th Parliament Session, would a Calendar of Events or Proposed Sittings be forthcoming to the Members? Thank you.
