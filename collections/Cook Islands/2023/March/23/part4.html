To the Honourable Speaker of the House, Greetings to you in the Name of our Lord Jesus.
Through you Mr Speaker, I would like to convey my regards to everyone in the House, including the Honourable Prime Minister, Deputy Prime Minister, Honourable Ministers, and Members of the Opposition side.
Through you Mr Speaker, I would also like to convey our thanks to the Honourable Minister of Tupapa/Maraerenga, Honourable George Angene, for supplying us with many things with no fees whatsoever, including a roaster, barbeque plates, petrol, water blast, solar equipment and many more.
We are truly gratified with his blessings and the Pukapuka Community of Rarotonga would like to thank him for his generosity.
God bless you Honourable Minister; you and your wife and your family.
This message is from Dr Kirianu Nio, Leader of the Pukapuka Community in Tupapa.
21 March 2023
To the Speaker of Parliament Rarotonga Nooroa Teipo President of the Rarotonga Bowling Club
Regarding: Solar Lights.
On behalf of Rarotonga Bowling Club Executive and all the members, I would like to extend to you our very sincere appreciation to the Honourable George Angene for the gift of 20 solar lights presented to the Rarotonga Bowling to assist the Club with lighting their bowling green.
Most times, our games go into the night and these lights will ensure games can be completed. These gifts came at the right time as the Club is looking for funding to replace our aging lights and power poles.
He was also instrumental in negotiating with Raro Welding to ensure that the lights are installed and they are now working and lighting our bowling green in the evenings since Friday the 18th of March 2023.
