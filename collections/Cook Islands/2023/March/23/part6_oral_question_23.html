Oral Question 23

HON. T. PUPUKE BROWNE: I stand to ask my question to those people responsible for the Cook Islands Party radio program. I recall about three weeks ago, the agreement was set for the improvement of the airport in Manihiki. The work for the upgrading of the airport in Manihiki can start.
But I have been asked by the landowners of whom I represent to ask this question in this House because there are news that has been mentioned on this program. Maybe the response from this question will confirm the truth about this matter. What I was told even though I did not listen to the program, but I was told that the blame was on the landowners and maybe the lawyers as well for the delay in the upgrade.
