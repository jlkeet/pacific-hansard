Oral Question 43

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker. Thank you for the question from one of my most favourite Members of the Opposition. The new Standing Orders Mr Speaker, provides for a Business Committee and also the establishment of a Bills, Petitions and Papers Committee and it is the role of these Committees to prepare a Calendar of Sittings and Bills that will be presented before the House. Of course, on these Committees are Members from each of our different parties that are within this House. I am sure we would be able to put together a Calendar of Sittings for this House during the year.
However, as a Member of this House for 12 years and as a Member of the Government, it is no surprise that on occasions, the Governments business will come in and interfere with the Parliamentary Sittings. And on occasion, the Prime Minister or Ministers may be required to attend meetings and be away, and that may require us to adjust our schedule as need be because on occasion, these invitations come at a very short notice.
