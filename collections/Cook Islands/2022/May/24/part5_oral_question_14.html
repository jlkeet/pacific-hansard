Oral Question 14

MRS S. NAPA: I have a Supplementary Question to the Minister of Health. To the Honourable Minister, I am aware that MOH has expended quite a sum of money during COVID on our dental clinic in Titikaveka, the only problem is that there is no dentist on our side.
The purpose of this clinic is to allow our people residing in this area of Takitumu, including Rutaki, to visit this clinic for dental treatment. If this facility is not being used, this property will deteriorate and all its equipment inside of it.
My question to the Honourable Minister is, will there be a dentist employed for this clinic of Takitumu and will MOH consider in utilizing this clinic in Titikaveka?
