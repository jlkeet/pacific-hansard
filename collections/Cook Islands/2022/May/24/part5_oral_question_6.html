Oral Question 6

MR W. HEATHER: Thank you Mr Speaker. Good morning to you and all Members of this House and the staff of Parliament, including all of our people listening in on our radio. I want to direct my question to the Prime Minister because he is the leader of our country and he is also the Minister Responsible for Finace.
My question is, in the past few days, we all know that there are eggs hatching in Arorangi from around Edgewater and from Blackrock to the school in Arorangi with eggs along the beaches. With eggs hatching in this area, even around restaurants and on the side of the main road, what can be done to sort this problem out because Ruaau have raised some concerns about this problem? That is my question Honourable Prime Minister, I will await for your favorable response. Thank you.
