Oral Question 18

MRS T. MATAPO: Good morning to everyone. I appreciate the answers from the Ministers. I rise on behalf of Outer Islands, especially the island of Mangaia. When I heard the discussion about the dental equipment in Titikaveka, it is a bit different for Mangaia because we have nothing. Honourable Minister, you were saying that you will carry out an investigation in this area, I just wanted to let you know that our dentist followed us to Rarotonga and if you can have the dental equipment checked. Last week, I went to the dental clinic and the machine they were using was leaking. As the Minister have mentioned about hygienic, this is different. The dental equipment works but is leaking onto patients.
