Oral Question 8

HON. M. BROWN: Thank you Mr Speaker, I pity this request from Puaikura. I think when we look at the Appropriation for this year, there is an allocation for our road development, nearly $7 million. At the moment, there is work on the road leading to the Avatiu valley. This is one of the worst roads on our islands and it needs to be improved.
When that is finished, we will begin from Turangi to the Church in Aroko. This project will finish in Titikaveka, an area where this project has not started yet and we will work our way towards Vaimaanga. After Titikaveka, we will make our way into Arorangi. The aim here is, the companies that are conducting this work, they want the job done and they do not want to sit around, in fact, they want push this work forward.
Therefore, this is a priority for the Government. When the work on one side of the road is completed, we will begin straight away with Puaikura. We know that Puaikura is a place where eggs are hatched for the country. Sometimes, there are bad eggs but sometimes, you good get good eggs. So, that is my reply to your question Honourable Member. Thank you Mr Speaker.
