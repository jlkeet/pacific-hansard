Oral Question 16

HON. R. TOKI-BROWN: Kia Orana everybody. Thank you to the Honourable Member of Titikaveka for the good question. Today, we struggle in recruiting dentists for our dental clinics. However, I am excited to inform everybody that we were able to recruit a dentist who is a Specialist as well. We have also discovered that his partner is also a dentist from overseas. They will be able to assist our dental department, not only for Rarotonga, but for the Outer Islands as well. Our plan for the dental clinic in your Constituency Honourable Member, we will look into utilising that clinic as the money used to renovate the clinic is from the Government.
