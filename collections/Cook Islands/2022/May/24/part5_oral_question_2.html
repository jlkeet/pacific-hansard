Oral Question 2

MR V. TUPA: Thank you Mr Speaker. My question is more of a request because I am not the type of person who is shy to ask for help. When I saw the Honourable Minister George Angene in his uniform, therefore my question will be directed to him. I can see he is wearing a green and gold uniform. My question to you Honourable Minister is, I heard that you will be putting up a dome and you have received a dome. I heard that this dome will be established in Tupapa/Maraerenga. Therefore, my question is, is it possible to get one for Matavera? Mr Speaker, if you look at Matavera, it is just a small village. But the thing I like about this village is the six different Denominations in it. This is what I wanted to ask, if a dome can be established in the village of Matavera. Thank you very much.
