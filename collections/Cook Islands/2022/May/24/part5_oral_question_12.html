Oral Question 12

HON. R. TOKI-BROWN: Thank you. Kia Orana Mr Speaker, Clerk and staff of Parliament, our Interpreters and our people listening in on our radio. Thank you to the Honourable Member of Ngatangiia for the question. Yes, the expenditure of the Ministry of Health (MOH) does not disregard the different parts that is controlled by MOH. I wanted to confirm to everybody today that we have received new equipment for our Hospital within the dental department. Before COVID arrived, we did some inspection in our dental clinics. But due to COVID, this work was postponed.
In reference to your query about the hygiene of this clinic, I wanted to inform you all that we have staff members allocated to clean our Hospital including Managers whose responsibility is to monitor their work. I am quite disappointed with your query but I will investigate this myself in rectifying this problem. Thank you very much.
