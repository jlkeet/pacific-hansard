Oral Question 30

MR A. NICHOLAS: Thank you Mr Speaker and all of us in this House and to the question from the Member. Maybe he is wondering why I have stood up to answer instead of the Prime Minister. It is because the Prime Minister have allocated this responsibility to me in recent times in looking at the progress of the project as mentioned by the Member.
In response to the query from the Member, yes just the other week we along with the Waste Management team met with two consultants from New Zealand. These two gentlemen have been working in the incinerator industry. These two presented their recommendations to us the team from Infrastructure Cook Islands and Waste Management. In this recommendation they put forward some thoughts on the incinerator as you have mentioned. The comments were accepted by the Waste Management and they were requested to return in August this year.
