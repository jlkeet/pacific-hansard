Oral Question 24

MR N. BAKER: My question is directed to the Prime Minister as he is the Minister Responsible for Finance. Before I do so, I would like to express a short story because it has been about five to six years that I have received a Bill from the Tax Department advising me to pay $84 thousand that I owe them and yet, I heard afterwards that taxes for some companies have been abolished.
My question then is, I heard that T&M is one of the companies in which their taxes has been cut. Is this true?
