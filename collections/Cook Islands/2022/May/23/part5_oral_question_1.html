Oral Question 1

MR V. TUPA: Kia Orana to all of us in the Mighty Name of our Saviour, here in Parliament. To you Mr Speaker, it has been some time since we have met in this Honourable House. My question is posed to the Honourable Minister, George Angene. I have heard and I have been told that you have conducted a new funeral services business. This has been broadcasted over the radio and you have taken control of this new funeral services business. Maybe it is true as I am unaware, I was informed about it and that is my question to you Honourable Minister, is it true that you have started a new funeral services business in hope to help those bury their loved ones?
