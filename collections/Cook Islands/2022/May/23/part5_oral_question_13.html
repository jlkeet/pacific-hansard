Oral Question 13

MRS S. NAPA: Kia Orana to you Mr Speaker, to all Honourable Members and to all the staff of Parliament. I would like to direct my question to the Honourable Prime Minister. I stand to ask the question on the shortness of time that was given to us as we were only given Book No. 1 and I have heard of some news circulating that we will debate on the Appropriation Bill today! There is also another news spreading around that Parliament will finish on Friday!
Can the Honourable Prime Minister indicate to us as to how long this Parliament Sitting will be?
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Honourable Member. I see the Honourable Prime Minister.
