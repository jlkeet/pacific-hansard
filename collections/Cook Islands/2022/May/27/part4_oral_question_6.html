Oral Question 6

HON. G. ANGENE: Thank you Mr Speaker and to all of the Members. Greetings to all our people listening in, watching on livestream throughout the world.
Before I ask my question, I would like to clarify my thoughts. My question is directed to the Leader of the Opposition. Yesterday when she made her statement, she referred to the Ministry of Justice and the Justices of the Peace.
This created a thought in my head to ask her this question. She mentioned that these Justices of the Peace could be Judges for our country. This is then the question to you Leader of the Opposition.
Why did you consider that these JPs will be suited for the Judge position and yet you are the lawyer among these people and this post better suits you? My question then is this; are you willing to
