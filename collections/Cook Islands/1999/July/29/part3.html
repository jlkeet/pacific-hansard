(CLERK OF PARLIAMENT EXIT THE CHAMBER)
The new Speaker elect.
SPEAKER-ELECT - HON. N. PUNA: Please be seated.The Honourable Members and to your loved ones, to all our friends and families inside the Chamber and outside, Kia Orana in the name of our Lord. I will not speak too long because we need to get on with the swearing in process but I would like to thank you all Honourable Members before I am to present myself to His Excellency for the swearing in. I would like to thank you Honourable Members for putting your trust and belief in me to take on this distinguished office.

29th July 1999
A special Greetings to our Coalition Government for the nomination in electing me as Speaker for this House. My Greetings to a former Speaker of this House to Papa Tuti Taringa and to other former Speakers such as the late Papa David Hosking and to the first Speaker of this Honourable House, to Mama Marguarite Story.
With the help of the Lord I will try and do the same as they did. I will now suspend Parliament and present myself to the Queen’s Representative to subscribe to the Oath of Allegiances and to lay claim to all the privileges of this Parliament. So this Parliament is now suspended until such time that we return from the Queen’s Representative.
