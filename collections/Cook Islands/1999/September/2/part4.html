If I can recall correctly it was set up in 1996 and before that the process of starting a business in this country was very bizarre and extremely contorted. It is the responsibility of the DIB to asses the people who are wishing to invest in our country, to conduct due diligent investigations into the history of those people who want to set up businesses in our country.
