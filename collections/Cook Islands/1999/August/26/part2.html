And now the motion for the sitting hours please, Deputy Prime Minister. Thank you.
HON. N. GEORGE: Mr Speaker, I move:
That this House observes the following sitting days and times For Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays the sitting hours will now be from 1.00pm to 5.00 p.m. with a break from 2.30pm to 3.00pm. And on Fridays from 10.00a.m. to 2.00pm with a break from 11.30am to 12.00 Noon.
Just by way of a brief explanation Mr Speaker, this means we are resorting back to the requirements of the Standing Orders that we have for the sitting of Parliament. During the last term of Parliament the sittings were reduced to three hours and there was no time allocated for a meal break. The sittings were reduced to three hours. Now that the economy has picked up we are now able to increase the sitting time back to where we were before. I also want to acknowledge the role played by the Elijah Communications which is the owner of the Radio Station. They have agreed to charge us for only half of our radio time and they have given us the other half in the name of freedom of speech.
Aside from that, I hope the Members have noticed the great improvements in the kitchen facilities during the breaks. These are the changes brought about by the Coalition Government to improve the standards in this House. I also would like to acknowledge the role played by the Member for Nikao-Panama, Ngamau Munokoa who has been subsidising food for the livelihood of the Members. Aunty Mau has been helping sustain the Members of this House by donating surplus food from her shop. So with these housekeeping matters in place we can look forward to outstanding debates in this House based entirely on the principles and merits and whatever result is needed to make the debate in this House of a high quality.
And of course, Mr Speaker, with your sparkling and gracious presence, we could expect the work of this House, the standard of this House to improve considerably. And I also want to advise you that I have consulted with the Leader of the Opposition about these changes and I am quite sure that they will also be giving unanimous support to us to approve it.
Thank you very much.
