PRAYER
MR SPEAKER took the Chair at 1.00 p.m.
MR SPEAKER: Please be seated Honourable Members.
Greetings to all Honourable Members who are present in this Chamber this afternoon. I am grateful to see all Honourable Members gathered in this House again. We would like to thank the servant of God Elder Totini who conducted our devotion. I have forgotten to mention the name of the preacher yesterday Danny Mataroa, so we would like to thank both of them.
I would like to remind all Honourable Members about our dress code when we come in to Parliament. We should be dressed in the proper manner and not just for the sake of wearing a suit without the shoes. Unless you have a sore on your foot then you are excused. This is the standard required of us. If you have other standard then that is your decision.
