(MUCH LAUGHTER)
Mr Speaker, these people are mocking me. That is why I don’t like the comments made earlier. They are misleading statements, which confuse our people, and are twisting their minds. It is blinding them from reality. I think this is all wrong. I’d like you to know, Mr Speaker, that I have always respected you.
Those are my views on this Motion, Mr Speaker. I don’t like this sort of Motion. What I really want to say is for the Referendum question to be clear so our people do not get confused. The question should be worded that the term be reduced to 4 or 3. However, I know the Prime Minister will get up and twist things around and say it is not possible. I think you know also, Mr Speaker, that there are a lot of people who prefer a 3-year term. Why do they want this? Because of the terrible mismanagement and the bad state that this country is in today.
