PARLIAMENT RESUMED
The Honourable Deputy Prime Minister.
HON. I. AKARURU: Mr Speaker, it is my pleasure once again to report that the Airport Authority Amendment Bill has passed through the Committee Stage without amendment.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Deputy Prime Minister.
The Question is:
That the Report be Adopted?
Report Adopted
We will now take the third reading. The Honourable Deputy Prime Minister.
HON. I. AKARURU: Mr Speaker, I move:
That the Airport Authority Amendment Bill be now Read a Third time.
MR SPEAKER: Thank you Deputy Prime Minister. May I call for a Seconder please?
Seconded by the Hon. Member T. Hewett
The Question is:
That the Motion be Agreed to?
Motion Agreed to
CLERK-AT-THE-TABLE: A Bill intituled: An Act to amend the Airport Authority Act 1985 to ensure consistency between its provisions and the provisions of the Cook Islands Investment Corporation Act 1998.
MR SPEAKER: And this concludes the Second and Third Readings of the Airport Authority Amendment Bill.
