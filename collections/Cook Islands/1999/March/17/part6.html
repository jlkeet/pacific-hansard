ORDERS OF THE DAY
Companies Amendment Bill, Second Reading. The Question is:
That the Bill be now Read a Second time?
The Honourable Minister of Education, Ngereteina Puna.
HON. N. PUNA: Thank you Mr Speaker. Kia Orana to you, to Honourable Members of the House and to our people listening in throughout the Cook Islands.
Last night we heard the Honourable Dr Terepai Maoate Leader of the Opposition saying that the economy of the country has gone right down into a bottomless pit. It is surprising that I am reading here that some of our business people are saying everything is alright. Let me read this writing to you.
“The display of economic buffoonery by the Opposition parties just shows us all how out of touch they are with reality, they have no credibility.”
This is what is being said about them. Thank you Mr Speaker.
