PUBLIC BILLS FOR PRESENTATION
Ports Authority Amendment Bill. The Honourable Minister Vaine Tairea.
CLERK AT THE TABLE: Short Title reads, Ports Authority Amendment Act 1999.
MR SPEAKER: Can the Honourable Minister name the date and time for the Second Reading of the Bill?
HON. V. TAIREA: Yes, Mr Speaker. Immediately after the completion of the Cook Islands Investment Amendment Bill.
MR SPEAKER: The Bill is now Ordered to come up for Second Reading immediately after the completion of the Cook Islands Investment Corporation Amendment Bill.
Airport Authority Amendment Bill. The Honourable Minister Vaine Tairea.
CLERK AT THE TABLE: Short Title reads, Airport Authority Amendment Act 1999.
MR SPEAKER: Can the Minister name the date and time of the Second Reading of the Bill?
HON. V. TAIREA: Thank you, Mr Speaker. After the completion of the Ports Authority Amendment Bill, Sir.
MR SPEAKER: The Bill is Ordered for Second Reading after the completion of the Ports Authority Amendment Bill.
